# Chapter 9: Operating Company Playbooks

## Introduction

The Operating Companies are the engines of the Empire. They are where the work happens and the money is made. While PNR Holdings manages the capital, these companies manage the customers.

This chapter provides the high-level "Playbook" for each of the primary operating divisions: RegIntel (Consulting), Notroom (Services), TaxEar (Tax), and the Directory Portfolio.

---

## 9.1 RegIntel Advisors - Consulting Operations

**Mission**: To monetize high-level expertise in healthcare, engineering, and UX research.

### Service Offerings
*   **Healthcare Regulatory Consulting**: Advising on compliance, FDA pathways, and quality systems.
*   **UX Research**: User testing, persona development, and interface optimization (leveraging NN/g certification).
*   **Engineering Consulting**: Technical problem solving and systems design.

### Operational Model
*   **Client Acquisition**: LinkedIn outreach, industry conferences, referrals.
*   **Pricing**: High-ticket project fees ($10k+) or monthly retainers ($5k+). *No hourly billing unless absolutely necessary.*
*   **Delivery**: Solo delivery initially, scaling to subcontractors for execution.

---

## 9.2 Notroom Services - Professional Services

**Mission**: To provide essential, high-volume transaction services to the real estate and legal industries.

### Service Lines
1.  **Mobile Notary**: In-person signing services for title companies.
2.  **Remote Online Notary (RON)**: Digital notarization for clients nationwide.
3.  **Transaction Coordination (TC)**: Managing real estate closings (SubTo focus).
4.  **Loan Signing Agent (NSA)**: Specialized mortgage document signings.

### Operational Model
*   **Marketing**: Signing service platforms (SigningOrder, Snapdocs), direct marketing to Title Companies, SEO for local search.
*   **Systems**: SuiteDash for intake and scheduling.
*   **Scaling**: Hire contract notaries for overflow work.

---

## 9.3 TaxEar Advisory - Tax Practice

**Mission**: To provide tax preparation, planning, and representation services.

### Service Lines
1.  **Tax Preparation**: 1040 (Individual), 1120-S (S-Corp), 1065 (Partnership).
2.  **Tax Planning**: Year-round strategy to reduce liability (REPS, Cost Seg, etc.).
3.  **IRS Representation**: Audit defense and collections negotiation (requires EA license).

### Operational Model
*   **Credential**: Enrolled Agent (EA) license is the barrier to entry.
*   **Software**: Professional tax software (e.g., Drake, ProConnect).
*   **Client Base**: Small business owners and real estate investors (cross-sell from Notroom/Directories).

---

## 9.4 Directory Companies - Digital Assets

**Mission**: To build a portfolio of niche directory websites that generate passive recurring revenue.

### The Portfolio Strategy
*   **Platform**: Brilliant Directories (100 licenses owned).
*   **Niche Selection**: High-value, fragmented industries (e.g., "Distressed Land Buyers," "Elder Care Facilities").
*   **Monetization**:
    *   **Listings**: Monthly subscription for premium placement.
    *   **Leads**: Selling lead data to members.
    *   **Ads**: Banner ads and sponsored content.

### Development Process
1.  **Select Niche**: Research search volume and competition.
2.  **Deploy Site**: Clone the "Master Template" on Brilliant Directories.
3.  **Seed Content**: Scrape/import initial free listings to populate the map.
4.  **Outreach**: Email listed businesses to claim/upgrade their profile.
5.  **Automate**: Setup auto-billing and email sequences.
