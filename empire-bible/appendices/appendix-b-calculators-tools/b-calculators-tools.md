# Appendix B: Calculators & Tools

## Introduction

This appendix lists the essential calculators and modeling tools used in the Dynasty. These tools are available in the **Dynasty Financial Tracker** (Excel/Google Sheets).

---

## Section B.1: ROI Calculator

**Purpose:** Evaluate the potential return on any investment (Marketing, Tech, Hiring).
**Formula:** `(Net Profit / Cost of Investment) x 100`
**Inputs:**
- Initial Cost
- Ongoing Costs
- Expected Revenue Increase
- Expected Cost Savings
- Time Horizon

---

## Section B.2: Tax Estimator

**Purpose:** Estimate quarterly tax payments to avoid penalties.
**Inputs:**
- YTD Net Income
- Projected Annual Income
- Effective Tax Rate (Federal + State)
- Safe Harbor Threshold (100% or 110% of last year's tax)

---

## Section B.3: Cash Flow Projector

**Purpose:** 13-Week rolling forecast to manage liquidity.
**Inputs:**
- Beginning Cash Balance
- Expected Cash In (AR, Sales)
- Expected Cash Out (AP, Payroll, Rent)
- Minimum Cash Reserve Target

---

## Section B.4: Valuation Models

**Purpose:** Estimate the current value of the business or a potential acquisition.
**Methods:**
- **SDE Multiple:** Seller Discretionary Earnings x Industry Multiple (2x-4x).
- **EBITDA Multiple:** Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization x Multiple (4x-8x).
- **DCF:** Discounted Cash Flow (for larger entities).

---

## Section B.5: Budget Templates

**Purpose:** Zero-based budgeting for annual planning.
**Tabs:**
- Revenue Assumptions
- COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
- OpEx (Operating Expenses)
- Capex (Capital Expenditures)
- Headcount Plan
