# Notary Services Rate Sheet

## Standard Mobile Notary
*   **Travel Fee**: $50 (up to 20 miles)
*   **Per Signature**: State regulated max (e.g., $10 or $15)
*   **Wait Time**: $15 per 15 minutes (after first 15 mins)

## Loan Signing Services (NSA)
*   **Refinance**: $150
*   **Purchase (Buyer/Seller)**: $150
*   **Reverse Mortgage**: $200
*   **HELOC**: $125
*   **Loan Modification**: $100
*   *Includes printing (up to 150 pages), travel, and scan-backs.*

## Remote Online Notary (RON)
*   **Single Document**: $25
*   **Additional Seal**: $10
*   **Loan Signing Package**: $100

## Surcharges
*   **Rush (Same Day)**: +$50
*   **After Hours (6pm-8am)**: +$50
*   **Weekend/Holiday**: +$50
*   **Jail/Hospital**: +$100
*   **Excessive Printing**: +$25 (over 150 pages)

---

**Terms**:
*   Payment due upon completion of signing.
*   Net-30 available for Title Companies with MSA.
*   Cancellation fee: $50 (if within 2 hours of appointment).
