# Facebook/Meta Ads Copy Template

## Purpose

This template provides frameworks for writing effective Facebook and Instagram ad copy that stops the scroll and drives action.

## Instructions

1. **Know Your Audience**: Write in their language
2. **Lead with Hook**: First 3 words must grab attention
3. **Tell a Story**: Use narrative to engage
4. **Visual First**: Copy supports the image/video
5. **Clear CTA**: Make the next step obvious

## Template Structure

### Facebook Feed Ad

**Primary Text** (125 characters for best engagement):
> [HOOK - First 3 words critical]
> 
> [STORY OR BENEFIT]
> 
> [CALL TO ACTION]

**Headline** (40 characters):
> [BENEFIT OR OFFER]

**Description** (30 characters):
> [SUPPORTING DETAIL]

**CTA Button**: [Learn More / Shop Now / Sign Up / etc.]

## Best Practices

- **Hook**: First 3 words determine if they keep reading
- **Emojis**: Use sparingly (1-2 per post max)
- **Length**: Shorter often performs better (under 125 chars)
- **Questions**: Questions in first line increase engagement
- **Urgency**: "Limited time," "Only X left" create urgency
