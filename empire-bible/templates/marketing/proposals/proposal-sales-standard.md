# Standard Sales Proposal Template

## Purpose

This template is used to present a formal offer to a prospective client. It bridges the gap between a sales conversation and a contract, outlining the value, solution, and price.

## Instructions

1.  **Customize**: Tailor the "Solution" and "Options" to the specific prospect.
2.  **Focus on Value**: Emphasize *outcomes* over *features*.
3.  **Keep it Clear**: Use bullet points and short paragraphs.
