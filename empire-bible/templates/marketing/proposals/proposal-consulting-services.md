# Consulting Services Proposal Template

## Purpose

This template is used to pitch consulting services to a prospective client. It outlines the problem, proposed solution, methodology, timeline, and investment.

## Instructions

1.  **Diagnose**: Clearly state the client's problem to show understanding.
2.  **Prescribe**: Outline your solution and why it works.
3.  **Price**: Present investment options clearly.
4.  **Proof**: Include testimonials or case studies.
