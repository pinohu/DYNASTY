# Case Study Template

## Purpose

Case studies are powerful marketing tools that provide social proof. This template helps structure a success story to highlight the problem, solution, and measurable results.

## Instructions

1.  **Interview**: Gather data from the client or project team.
2.  **Metrics**: Focus on hard numbers (revenue gained, time saved).
3.  **Format**: Use the "Challenge -> Solution -> Result" structure.
