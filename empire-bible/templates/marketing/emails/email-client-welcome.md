# Client Welcome Email Template

## Purpose

This template provides a professional welcome email to send to new clients immediately after contract execution. The email sets a positive tone, provides essential information, and guides clients through the next steps.

## Instructions

1. **Customize**: Replace all [PLACEHOLDERS] with actual information
2. **Personalize**: Add specific details about the client's service
3. **Review**: Ensure all links and contact information are correct
4. **Send**: Send within 24 hours of contract execution
5. **Follow Up**: Track email opens and follow up if no response

## Best Practices

- **Timing**: Send within 24 hours of contract execution
- **Tone**: Warm, professional, and enthusiastic
- **Clarity**: Clear next steps and expectations
- **Personalization**: Customize for each client
- **Action-Oriented**: Include specific actions for the client
- **Support**: Make it easy to get help

## Follow-Up

- Track email opens and clicks
- Follow up if no response within 3 days
- Send reminder if kickoff call not scheduled within 5 days
- Collect feedback on welcome experience
