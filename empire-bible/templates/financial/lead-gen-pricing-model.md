# Lead Generation Pricing Model

## Lead Categories

### Tier 1: High Value (Urgent/Large Projects)
*   **Examples**: Roof Replacement, HVAC Install, Tax Audit Defense.
*   **Price Per Lead**: $50 - $100
*   **Exclusive Price**: $150+

### Tier 2: Medium Value (Standard Services)
*   **Examples**: Plumbing Repair, Notary Signing, Lawn Maintenance.
*   **Price Per Lead**: $20 - $40
*   **Exclusive Price**: $75

### Tier 3: Low Value (Small Tasks)
*   **Examples**: Dog Walking, House Cleaning (One-time).
*   **Price Per Lead**: $5 - $15
*   **Exclusive Price**: $30

---

## Lead Delivery Rules
1.  **Shared Leads**: Sold to a maximum of 3 providers.
2.  **Exclusive Leads**: Sold to 1 provider only.
3.  **Refund Policy**: We refund leads with bad phone numbers or out-of-area requests. We do *not* refund leads just because they didn't buy.

## Bulk Packages
*   **Starter Pack**: 10 Leads (5% Discount)
*   **Pro Pack**: 50 Leads (15% Discount)
*   **Enterprise Pack**: 100 Leads (25% Discount)
