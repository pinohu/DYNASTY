# Monthly Balance Sheet Template

## Purpose

This template provides a comprehensive monthly Balance Sheet for tracking assets, liabilities, and equity. Use this template to monitor financial position and track changes over time.

## Instructions

1. **Select Period**: Enter the month and year for the statement
2. **Enter Assets**: Record all assets by category
3. **Enter Liabilities**: Record all liabilities by category
4. **Calculate Equity**: Calculate equity (Assets - Liabilities)
5. **Verify Balance**: Ensure Assets = Liabilities + Equity
6. **Compare to Prior Period**: Compare to previous month's balance sheet
7. **Review Changes**: Analyze significant changes in account balances
8. **Distribute**: Share with management and stakeholders

## Notes

- **Timing**: Prepare monthly balance sheet within 5-10 business days after month-end

- **Accuracy**: Ensure all accounts are reconciled and balances are accurate

- **Balance Check**: Assets must equal Liabilities + Equity - if not, find the error

- **Comparison**: Always compare to prior period to identify trends and changes

- **Ratios**: Calculate key ratios to assess financial health

- **Reconciliation**: Reconcile all balance sheet accounts monthly
