# Annual Profit & Loss Statement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a full-year summary of financial performance. It is essential for tax preparation, annual reviews, and strategic planning.

## Instructions

1.  **Compile Data**: Aggregate data from all 12 months.
2.  **Adjustments**: Include year-end adjustments (depreciation, amortization, accruals).
3.  **Tax Prep**: Use this as the basis for tax filings.
