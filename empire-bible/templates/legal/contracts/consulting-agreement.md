# Consulting Agreement Template

## Purpose

This template is designed for consulting engagements where the provider offers expert advice, strategy, or specialized knowledge. It differs from a general services agreement by focusing on advisory roles and intellectual capital.

**IMPORTANT**: Review with legal counsel.

## Instructions

1.  **Define Scope**: Clearly articulate the consulting objectives and deliverables.
2.  **IP Rights**: Pay close attention to IP ownership of strategies and models developed.
3.  **Non-Compete**: Consider if a non-compete clause is appropriate (check state laws).

## Template

```markdown
# CONSULTING AGREEMENT

**Date**: [DATE]

**Consultant**: [CONSULTANT NAME] ("Consultant")
**Client**: [CLIENT NAME] ("Client")

## 1. ENGAGEMENT

Client hereby engages Consultant to provide the consulting services described in **Exhibit A** ("Services").

## 2. PERFORMANCE

Consultant shall perform the Services to the best of their ability, in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.

## 3. COMPENSATION

Client shall pay Consultant as follows:
- Rate: $[AMOUNT] per [HOUR/DAY/PROJECT]
- Retainer: $[AMOUNT] (if applicable)
- Payment Schedule: [DETAILS]

## 4. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee of Client. Consultant is responsible for all taxes and benefits.

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Consultant acknowledges that they may have access to Client's confidential information. Consultant agrees not to disclose or use such information except for the benefit of Client.

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Any reports, strategies, or specific work product created solely for Client shall be the property of Client. Consultant retains ownership of their pre-existing methodologies, frameworks, and tools.

## 7. NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this Agreement and for [NUMBER] months thereafter, Consultant shall not solicit Client's employees or customers.

## 8. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this Agreement with [NUMBER] days' written notice. Client shall pay for all Services performed up to the date of termination.

## 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Consultant's liability shall be limited to the amount of fees paid by Client under this Agreement.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE].

---

**Signatures**:

Consultant: __________________________ Date: __________

Client: ______________________________ Date: __________

---

## EXHIBIT A: SERVICES

[Detailed description of consulting services]
```
