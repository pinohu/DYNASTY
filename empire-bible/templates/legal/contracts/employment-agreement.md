# Employment Agreement Template

## Purpose

This agreement is for hiring W-2 employees. It outlines salary, benefits, duties, and "at-will" status.

**IMPORTANT**: Employment laws vary significantly by state. This is a general template and must be reviewed by local counsel.

## Instructions

1.  **Position**: Define the role and reporting structure.
2.  **Compensation**: State salary and pay frequency.
3.  **At-Will**: Confirm "at-will" employment (can be terminated at any time).
4.  **Restrictive Covenants**: Non-compete/Non-solicit clauses (check state enforceability).

## Template

```markdown
# EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

**Date**: [DATE]

**Employer**: [COMPANY NAME]
**Employee**: [EMPLOYEE NAME]

## 1. POSITION AND DUTIES

Employer hires Employee for the position of [JOB TITLE]. Employee agrees to perform the duties associated with this position and report to [MANAGER TITLE]. Employee shall devote their full business time and attention to the Employer.

## 2. COMPENSATION

2.1 **Salary**. Employee shall receive a base salary of $[AMOUNT] per year, payable in accordance with Employer's standard payroll schedule.
2.2 **Benefits**. Employee shall be eligible to participate in standard benefit plans offered by Employer (e.g., health insurance, 401k), subject to plan terms.
2.3 **Bonus**. Employee [IS/IS NOT] eligible for discretionary bonuses based on performance.

## 3. AT-WILL EMPLOYMENT

Employee's employment is "at-will." This means that either Employee or Employer may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND IP

4.1 **Confidentiality**. Employee agrees not to disclose Employer's confidential information or trade secrets during or after employment.
4.2 **Intellectual Property**. All work created by Employee during employment belongs to Employer.

## 5. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS

5.1 **Non-Solicitation**. During employment and for [NUMBER] months after termination, Employee shall not solicit Employer's customers or employees.
5.2 **Non-Compete** (Check State Law). During employment and for [NUMBER] months after termination, Employee shall not engage in a competing business within [GEOGRAPHIC AREA].

## 6. TERMINATION

Upon termination, Employer shall pay all accrued salary and unused vacation time (if required by state law). Employee shall return all Employer property.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

**SIGNATURES**:

**Employer**:
__________________________
[NAME], [TITLE]

**Employee**:
__________________________
[EMPLOYEE NAME]
```
