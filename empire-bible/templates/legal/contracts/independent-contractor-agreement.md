# Independent Contractor Agreement Template

## Purpose

This agreement is for hiring freelancers, consultants, or virtual assistants (VAs). It clearly establishes that the worker is *not* an employee, which is critical for tax and liability purposes.

## Instructions

1.  **Scope**: Define the specific services in Exhibit A.
2.  **Status**: Reinforce independent status (no taxes withheld, no benefits).
3.  **IP**: Ensure "Work for Hire" language is present so you own the output.

## Template

```markdown
# INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

**Date**: [DATE]

**Company**: [COMPANY NAME]
**Contractor**: [CONTRACTOR NAME]

## 1. SERVICES

Contractor agrees to perform the services described in **Exhibit A** (the "Services"). Contractor shall determine the method, details, and means of performing the Services.

## 2. COMPENSATION

Company shall pay Contractor as follows:
-   **Rate**: $[AMOUNT] per [HOUR/PROJECT]
-   **Payment Terms**: Invoices payable within [NUMBER] days.

## 3. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR RELATIONSHIP

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee.
-   Contractor is responsible for all federal, state, and local taxes.
-   Contractor is not eligible for Company benefits (health insurance, PTO, etc.).
-   Company will not withhold taxes from payments.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (WORK FOR HIRE)

4.1 **Ownership**. All work product created by Contractor for Company ("Work Product") shall be considered a "work made for hire" and shall be the sole property of Company.
4.2 **Assignment**. If any Work Product does not qualify as a work made for hire, Contractor hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in such Work Product to Company.

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Contractor agrees to keep all Company information confidential and not to disclose it to third parties or use it for any purpose other than performing the Services.

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

6.1 **Term**. This Agreement commences on [START DATE] and continues until [END DATE] or completion of Services.
6.2 **Termination**. Either party may terminate this Agreement with [NUMBER] days' written notice.

## 7. INDEMNIFICATION

Contractor agrees to indemnify Company against any claims arising out of Contractor's negligence, misconduct, or breach of this Agreement.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].
```
