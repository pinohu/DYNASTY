# Client Services Agreement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a standard Client Services Agreement for professional services. It establishes the legal relationship between the service provider and the client, defining the scope of work, payment terms, and legal protections.

**IMPORTANT**: This template must be reviewed and customized by qualified legal counsel before execution.

## Instructions

1.  **Identify Parties**: Replace [PROVIDER] and [CLIENT] with actual names.
2.  **Define Services**: Clearly describe the scope of work in Schedule A.
3.  **Set Fees**: Specify fees and payment terms in Schedule B.
4.  **Customize Terms**: Adjust liability, indemnification, and termination clauses as needed.
5.  **Execute**: Have authorized representatives sign the agreement.

## Template

```markdown
# CLIENT SERVICES AGREEMENT

**Agreement Date**: [DATE]
**Effective Date**: [DATE]
**Agreement Number**: [NUMBER]

## PARTIES

**Service Provider** ("Provider"):
[PROVIDER NAME]
[ADDRESS]
[EMAIL]

**Client** ("Client"):
[CLIENT NAME]
[ADDRESS]
[EMAIL]

## 1. SERVICES

Provider agrees to perform the services described in **Schedule A** (the "Services") in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

2.1 **Term**. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until [DATE] or until the Services are completed, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

2.2 **Termination**. Either party may terminate this Agreement:
(a) For convenience upon [NUMBER] days' written notice;
(b) Immediately for cause if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within [NUMBER] days of notice.

## 3. COMPENSATION

3.1 **Fees**. Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in **Schedule B**.

3.2 **Expenses**. Client shall reimburse Provider for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in performing the Services.

3.3 **Payment Terms**. Invoices are due within [NUMBER] days of receipt. Late payments shall incur interest at [PERCENTAGE]% per month.

## 4. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Provider is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 **Pre-existing IP**. Each party retains ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property.

5.2 **Deliverables**. Upon full payment, Provider assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the specific deliverables created for Client under this Agreement ("Deliverables"). Provider retains the right to use its general knowledge, skills, and experience.

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to keep confidential all non-public information disclosed by the other party and to use such information only for the purposes of this Agreement.

## 7. WARRANTIES AND DISCLAIMER

7.1 **Warranty**. Provider warrants that Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.

7.2 **Disclaimer**. EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, PROVIDER MAKES NO WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED.

## 8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. PROVIDER'S TOTAL LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID UNDER THIS AGREEMENT IN THE [NUMBER] MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

## 9. INDEMNIFICATION

Client agrees to indemnify and hold Provider harmless from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from Client's use of the Deliverables or breach of this Agreement.

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

10.1 **Governing Law**. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [STATE].

10.2 **Dispute Resolution**. Disputes shall be resolved by [MEDIATION/ARBITRATION] in [LOCATION].

10.3 **Entire Agreement**. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
```
