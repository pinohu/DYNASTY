# General Partnership Agreement Template

## Purpose

This template establishes a General Partnership between two or more parties. It outlines capital contributions, profit sharing, management duties, and dissolution procedures.

**IMPORTANT**: Partnerships carry joint and several liability. Legal review is critical.

## Instructions

1.  **Partners**: Identify all partners.
2.  **Capital**: Specify initial contributions.
3.  **Profits/Losses**: Define distribution percentages.
4.  **Management**: Clarify decision-making authority.

## Template

```markdown
# GENERAL PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Date**: [DATE]

**Partners**:
1. [PARTNER 1 NAME]
2. [PARTNER 2 NAME]

## 1. NAME AND BUSINESS

The name of the partnership shall be [PARTNERSHIP NAME]. The principal business is [BUSINESS DESCRIPTION].

## 2. TERM

The partnership shall commence on [DATE] and continue until dissolved by mutual agreement or operation of law.

## 3. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

The Partners shall contribute the following capital:
- Partner 1: $[AMOUNT]
- Partner 2: $[AMOUNT]

## 4. PROFIT AND LOSS

Net profits and losses shall be divided as follows:
- Partner 1: [PERCENTAGE]%
- Partner 2: [PERCENTAGE]%

## 5. MANAGEMENT

Each Partner shall have an equal voice in the management of the partnership business. Major decisions require [UNANIMOUS/MAJORITY] consent.

## 6. BANKING

All partnership funds shall be deposited in the partnership's name at [BANK NAME]. Checks require the signature of [NUMBER] Partner(s).

## 7. WITHDRAWAL AND DEATH

In the event of a Partner's withdrawal or death, the remaining Partner(s) shall have the option to purchase the interest of the withdrawing or deceased Partner.

## 8. DISSOLUTION

Upon dissolution, the partnership assets shall be liquidated, debts paid, and remaining surplus distributed to Partners in accordance with their capital accounts.

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes shall be settled by arbitration in [STATE].

---

**Signatures**:

Partner 1: __________________________ Date: __________

Partner 2: __________________________ Date: __________
```
