# LLC Operating Agreement Template (Manager-Managed)

## Purpose
This is the governing contract for a Wyoming Limited Liability Company. It defines ownership, management, and rules.

## Article I: Organization
1.  **Formation**: The Company was formed by filing Articles of Organization with the Wyoming Secretary of State.
2.  **Purpose**: To engage in any lawful act or activity.
3.  **Term**: Perpetual.

## Article II: Members & Capital
1.  **Members**: The owners listed in Exhibit A.
2.  **Initial Capital**: Members have contributed the capital listed in Exhibit A.
3.  **Liability**: Members are not personally liable for the debts of the Company.

## Article III: Management
1.  **Manager-Managed**: The Company shall be managed by one or more Managers.
2.  **Authority**: The Manager has full, exclusive, and complete authority to manage the Company. Members have no voting rights on day-to-day operations.
3.  **Manager**: [Name of Manager] is appointed as the initial Manager.

## Article IV: Distributions
1.  **Discretionary**: Distributions of Net Cash Flow shall be made at the discretion of the Manager.
2.  **Pro Rata**: Distributions shall be made to Members in proportion to their Percentage Interests.

## Article V: Transfer of Interests
1.  **Restriction**: No Member may transfer their interest without the consent of the Manager.
2.  **Right of First Refusal**: The Company has the right to match any offer to buy a Member's interest.
3.  **Poison Pill**: If a Member becomes bankrupt or their interest is attached by a creditor, they immediately lose all voting rights and become a purely economic interest holder.

## Article VI: Dissolution
The Company shall dissolve only upon the written consent of all Members or the entry of a decree of judicial dissolution.
