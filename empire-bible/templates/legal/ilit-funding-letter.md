# ILIT Funding Letter

## Purpose
To document a gift to the Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust (ILIT) for the purpose of paying insurance premiums.

**Date**: __________________________
**To**: [Trustee Name]
**From**: [Grantor Name]
**Re**: Gift to [ILIT Name]

---

Dear Trustee,

I am enclosing a check (or confirming a wire transfer) in the amount of $[Amount] made payable to the [ILIT Name].

This transfer is an irrevocable gift to the Trust. It is my intention that this gift qualify for the annual exclusion from federal gift tax.

Please notify the beneficiaries of their right to withdraw their share of this contribution (Crummey Notice) immediately.

Once the withdrawal period has lapsed, please use these funds to pay the premium on Policy #[Policy Number] with [Insurance Carrier].

Sincerely,

__________________________
[Grantor Signature]
