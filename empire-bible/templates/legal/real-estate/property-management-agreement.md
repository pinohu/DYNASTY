# Property Management Agreement Template

## Purpose

This agreement establishes the relationship between a property owner and a property management company (or individual manager). It defines the manager's authority, duties, and compensation.

## Instructions

1.  **Authority**: Define what the manager can do (sign leases, spend money on repairs).
2.  **Fees**: Specify management fees (usually % of rent) and other fees (leasing fee, renewal fee).
3.  **Spending Limit**: Set a cap on repair costs the manager can authorize without owner approval.

## Template

```markdown
# PROPERTY MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT

**Date**: [DATE]

**Owner**: [OWNER NAME/ENTITY]
**Manager**: [MANAGEMENT COMPANY NAME]

**1. APPOINTMENT**
Owner appoints Manager as the exclusive agent to manage, lease, and operate the property located at:
[PROPERTY ADDRESS] (the "Property").

**2. TERM**
This Agreement shall commence on [START DATE] and continue for [NUMBER] year(s). It shall automatically renew unless terminated by either party with [NUMBER] days' written notice.

**3. MANAGER'S RESPONSIBILITIES**
Manager agrees to:
(a) Market the Property and screen prospective tenants.
(b) Execute leases and renewals on Owner's behalf.
(c) Collect rents and security deposits.
(d) Coordinate maintenance and repairs.
(e) Maintain accurate financial records and provide monthly reports to Owner.
(f) Handle tenant relations and evictions (if necessary).

**4. OWNER'S RESPONSIBILITIES**
Owner agrees to:
(a) Maintain property insurance (liability and hazard).
(b) Provide funds for necessary repairs and operating expenses.
(c) Indemnify Manager against claims arising from the Property (except for Manager's negligence).

**5. COMPENSATION**
Owner shall pay Manager:
-   **Management Fee**: [PERCENTAGE]% of gross monthly rent collected.
-   **Leasing Fee**: $[AMOUNT] or [PERCENTAGE]% of first month's rent for new tenant placement.
-   **Renewal Fee**: $[AMOUNT] for lease renewals.

**6. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT**
Manager may pay operating expenses from collected rents. If expenses exceed income, Owner shall reimburse Manager within [NUMBER] days.

**7. SPENDING AUTHORITY**
Manager is authorized to spend up to $[AMOUNT] for any single repair or maintenance item without prior Owner approval.
*Exception*: Emergency repairs necessary to protect the Property or tenant safety do not require prior approval.

**8. BANKING**
Manager shall hold all security deposits in a separate escrow account in compliance with state law. Rents shall be deposited into a trust account.

**9. TERMINATION**
Upon termination, Manager shall transfer all records, funds, and keys to Owner within [NUMBER] days.

**10. GOVERNING LAW**
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

---

**SIGNATURES**:

**Owner**:
__________________________
[NAME]
Date: __________

**Manager**:
__________________________
[NAME]
Date: __________
```
