# Residential Lease Agreement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a comprehensive residential lease agreement for renting property. It covers rent, security deposit, tenant responsibilities, and landlord rights.

**IMPORTANT**: Landlord-tenant law varies by state. This template must be customized by an attorney familiar with local laws.

## Instructions

1. **Complete Property Details**: Address, unit number, square footage
2. **Set Rent Amount**: Monthly rent and due date
3. **Security Deposit**: Amount and conditions for return
4. **Lease Term**: Start date, end date, renewal options
5. **Tenant Screening**: Reference checks, credit check, income verification
6. **Review with Attorney**: Must be reviewed by qualified counsel

- [ ] Automatically renew month-to-month unless either party gives [NUMBER] days' written notice

## RENT AND PAYMENTS

### 3. Monthly Rent

Tenant shall pay monthly rent of **$[AMOUNT]** per month, due on the [DAY] of each month.

### 4. Late Fees

If rent is not received by the [DAY] of the month, Tenant shall pay a late fee of **$[AMOUNT]** or [PERCENTAGE]% of monthly rent, whichever is greater.

### 5. Payment Method

Rent shall be paid by:
- [ ] Check mailed to [ADDRESS]
- [ ] Electronic transfer to [ACCOUNT]
- [ ] Online portal at [URL]
- [ ] [OTHER METHOD]

### 6. Security Deposit

Tenant has paid a security deposit of **$[AMOUNT]** which shall be held by Landlord and returned within [NUMBER] days after termination, less any deductions for:
- Unpaid rent
- Damage beyond normal wear and tear
- Cleaning costs (if property not left clean)
- Other charges permitted by law

---

## USE AND OCCUPANCY

### 7. Permitted Use

The Property shall be used solely as a private residence for Tenant and the following occupants:
- [LIST OCCUPANTS - names and relationship]

**Maximum Occupancy**: [NUMBER] persons

### 8. Prohibited Activities

Tenant shall not:
- Use Property for any illegal purpose
- Disturb neighbors or violate noise ordinances
- Keep pets without written permission (if applicable)
- Smoke in the Property (if non-smoking property)
- Make alterations without written consent
- Sublet or assign without written consent

---

## TENANT OBLIGATIONS

### 9. Maintenance and Repairs

Tenant shall:
- Keep Property clean and sanitary
- Dispose of trash properly
- Maintain plumbing fixtures
- Replace light bulbs and batteries
- Maintain smoke detectors
- Report needed repairs promptly
- [OTHER MAINTENANCE OBLIGATIONS]

### 10. Utilities

Tenant shall pay for:
- [ ] Electricity
- [ ] Gas
- [ ] Water/Sewer
- [ ] Trash
- [ ] Internet/Cable
- [OTHER UTILITIES]

Landlord shall pay for: [LIST LANDLORD-PAID UTILITIES]

### 11. Insurance

Tenant is required to maintain renter's insurance with minimum coverage of $[AMOUNT] for personal property and liability.

---

## LANDLORD OBLIGATIONS

### 12. Habitability

Landlord shall maintain Property in habitable condition and comply with all building codes and health regulations.

### 13. Repairs

Landlord shall make necessary repairs to maintain habitability, including:
- Structural repairs
- Plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems
- Appliances provided by Landlord
- [OTHER REPAIRS]

### 14. Entry

Landlord may enter Property:
- With [NUMBER] hours' notice for inspections, repairs, or showings
- In case of emergency without notice
- At reasonable times for other purposes permitted by law

---

## DEFAULT AND TERMINATION

### 15. Tenant Default

Tenant shall be in default if:
- Rent is not paid when due
- Tenant violates any term of this Lease
- Tenant abandons the Property
- Tenant engages in illegal activity

### 16. Remedies

Upon default, Landlord may:
- Terminate this Lease
- Evict Tenant
- Recover unpaid rent and damages
- Exercise other remedies available at law

### 17. Early Termination

If Tenant terminates early:
- Tenant shall pay rent until Property is re-rented or Lease Term ends
- Tenant shall pay reasonable re-leasing costs
- Security deposit may be forfeited

---

## GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 18. Entire Agreement

This Lease constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

### 19. Governing Law

This Lease shall be governed by the laws of [STATE].

### 20. Severability

If any provision is invalid, remaining provisions shall remain in effect.
