# Trustee Letter Template

## Purpose

This template serves as a formal record of a Trustee's decision or action. It is used to document discretionary distributions, investment changes, or administrative decisions, providing an audit trail for fiduciary compliance.

## Instructions

1.  **Topic**: Clearly state the subject of the decision.
2.  **Authority**: Reference the trust provision granting authority for the action.
3.  **Factors**: List the factors considered (e.g., beneficiary needs, trust liquidity).
4.  **Decision**: State the final decision clearly.

## Template

```markdown
# TRUSTEE DECISION LETTER

**Trust**: [TRUST NAME]
**Date**: [DATE]
**Trustee**: [TRUSTEE NAME]

## SUBJECT: [DECISION TOPIC, e.g., Discretionary Distribution to Beneficiary]

## BACKGROUND

The Trustee has received a request from [BENEFICIARY NAME] for a distribution of $[AMOUNT] for the purpose of [PURPOSE].

## AUTHORITY

Under Article [NUMBER] of the Trust Agreement, the Trustee has the discretion to make distributions for the beneficiary's health, education, maintenance, and support (HEMS).

## FACTORS CONSIDERED

In making this determination, the Trustee has considered:
1. The purpose of the request ([PURPOSE]).
2. The current value and liquidity of the Trust assets.
3. The beneficiary's other resources (if required by Trust).
4. The interests of remainder beneficiaries.

## DECISION

Based on the foregoing, the Trustee hereby **APPROVES** the distribution of $[AMOUNT].

## ACTION PLAN

1. The Trustee will liquidate [ASSET] to generate necessary cash (if applicable).
2. Payment will be made directly to [PROVIDER/BENEFICIARY] via [CHECK/WIRE].
3. This transaction will be recorded in the Trust's accounting records.

---

**Signed**:

__________________________
[TRUSTEE NAME], Trustee
```
