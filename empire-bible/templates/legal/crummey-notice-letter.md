# Crummey Notice Letter Template

## Purpose
Use this letter to notify a beneficiary (or their guardian) that a gift has been made to the trust and they have a right to withdraw it. This is critical for qualifying for the Annual Gift Tax Exclusion.

**Date**: __________________________

**To**: [Beneficiary Name]
**From**: [Trustee Name]
**Re**: Notice of Right of Withdrawal - [Trust Name]

---

Dear [Beneficiary Name],

This letter is to notify you that a contribution has been made to the **[Trust Name]** on **[Date of Deposit]** in the amount of **$[Amount]**.

Under the terms of the Trust Agreement (Article [Number]), you have the absolute right to withdraw this amount (or a portion thereof) for your own benefit. This withdrawal right will lapse and expire **30 days** from the date of this letter.

If you wish to exercise this right and withdraw the funds, please notify me in writing at the address below before the 30-day period expires. If you do not wish to withdraw the funds, no action is required on your part, and the funds will remain in the Trust to be administered according to its terms.

Sincerely,

__________________________
[Trustee Name], Trustee
