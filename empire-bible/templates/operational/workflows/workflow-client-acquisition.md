# Client Acquisition Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow defines the end-to-end process for acquiring new clients, from initial lead generation through contract signing and onboarding. It ensures consistency and maximizes conversion rates.

## Scope

Applies to all sales and business development activities for [COMPANY NAME].

## Workflow Steps

### Phase 1: Lead Generation

1. **Lead Sources**
   - [ ] Inbound marketing (website, SEO, content)
   - [ ] Outbound prospecting (cold calls, emails)
   - [ ] Referrals from existing clients
   - [ ] Networking events and conferences
   - [ ] Partnerships and alliances

2. **Lead Capture**
   - [ ] Lead enters CRM system
   - [ ] Lead source tagged
   - [ ] Initial lead score assigned
   - [ ] Automated welcome email sent

### Phase 2: Qualification

1. **Initial Contact**
   - [ ] Sales rep assigned (if not automated)
   - [ ] Initial outreach within [NUMBER] hours
   - [ ] Discovery call scheduled

2. **Discovery Call**
   - [ ] Understand prospect's needs
   - [ ] Identify decision-makers
   - [ ] Assess budget and timeline
   - [ ] Determine fit with services
   - [ ] **Qualification Criteria**:
     - [ ] Has need/problem we solve
     - [ ] Has budget
     - [ ] Has authority to buy
     - [ ] Has timeline

3. **Qualification Decision**
   - **Qualified**: Proceed to Phase 3
   - **Not Qualified**: Nurture in marketing sequence or disqualify

### Phase 3: Proposal & Presentation

1. **Proposal Development**
   - [ ] Customize proposal template
   - [ ] Include specific solution for prospect
   - [ ] Set pricing and terms
   - [ ] Prepare presentation materials

2. **Proposal Delivery**
   - [ ] Send proposal via email
   - [ ] Schedule presentation meeting (if needed)
   - [ ] Follow up within [NUMBER] days

3. **Presentation (if required)**
   - [ ] Present solution
   - [ ] Address questions and objections
   - [ ] Discuss next steps

### Phase 4: Negotiation & Closing

1. **Handle Objections**
   - [ ] Listen to concerns
   - [ ] Address each objection
   - [ ] Provide additional information if needed

2. **Negotiate Terms**
   - [ ] Discuss pricing (if needed)
   - [ ] Adjust scope (if needed)
   - [ ] Finalize contract terms

3. **Close**
   - [ ] Send final contract
   - [ ] Obtain signatures
   - [ ] Confirm receipt of signed contract
   - [ ] Celebrate win!

### Phase 5: Onboarding Handoff

1. **Internal Handoff**
   - [ ] Notify operations team
   - [ ] Transfer client information to CRM
   - [ ] Schedule kickoff meeting
   - [ ] Assign account manager

2. **Client Onboarding**
   - [ ] Send welcome email
   - [ ] Schedule kickoff call
   - [ ] Begin onboarding process (see Client Onboarding SOP)

## Common Objections & Responses

### "We don't have budget"
**Response**: "I understand budget is always a consideration. What would need to happen for this to become a priority? What's the cost of not addressing [THEIR PROBLEM]?"

### "We need to think about it"
**Response**: "I understand you want to think it over. What specific questions or concerns do you have that I can address?"

### "Your price is too high"
**Response**: "I understand price is important. Let me show you the ROI - [CLIENT NAME] achieved [RESULT] which saved them $[AMOUNT]. What would that kind of result be worth to you?"
