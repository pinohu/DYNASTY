# Monthly Close Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist ensures all monthly closing tasks are completed accurately and timely. Use this checklist to close the books each month and prepare financial statements.

## Instructions

1. **Start Early**: Begin closing process 2-3 days before month-end
2. **Follow Sequence**: Complete tasks in order (some depend on others)
3. **Document**: Check off items as completed and note any issues
4. **Review**: Review completed checklist before finalizing close
5. **File**: Keep completed checklist with monthly records

## Checklist

### Pre-Close Preparation (Days -3 to -1)

- [ ] Review prior month's close for any open items
- [ ] Notify team of upcoming close deadline
- [ ] Gather necessary documents and reports
- [ ] Review any known issues or transactions
- [ ] Prepare closing schedule and assign tasks

### Day 1: Transaction Entry and Review

#### Revenue
- [ ] Record all revenue transactions for the month
- [ ] Verify all invoices have been issued
- [ ] Review accounts receivable aging
- [ ] Record any accrued revenue
- [ ] Reconcile revenue accounts

#### Expenses
- [ ] Record all expense transactions for the month
- [ ] Verify all vendor bills have been entered
- [ ] Review accounts payable aging
- [ ] Record any accrued expenses
- [ ] Reconcile expense accounts

#### Payroll
- [ ] Process payroll for the month
- [ ] Record payroll journal entries
- [ ] Record payroll tax accruals
- [ ] Verify payroll tax deposits
- [ ] Reconcile payroll accounts

#### Cash
- [ ] Record all cash transactions
- [ ] Post all bank deposits
- [ ] Post all bank withdrawals
- [ ] Record bank fees and charges
- [ ] Reconcile petty cash

### Day 2: Reconciliations

#### Bank Reconciliations
- [ ] Reconcile operating bank account
- [ ] Reconcile savings account
- [ ] Reconcile credit card accounts
- [ ] Reconcile loan accounts
- [ ] Document all reconciling items
- [ ] Clear old reconciling items (if appropriate)

#### Account Reconciliations
- [ ] Reconcile accounts receivable
- [ ] Reconcile accounts payable
- [ ] Reconcile inventory (if applicable)
- [ ] Reconcile prepaid expenses
- [ ] Reconcile accrued liabilities
- [ ] Reconcile fixed assets
- [ ] Reconcile depreciation

#### Intercompany Reconciliations
- [ ] Reconcile intercompany receivables
- [ ] Reconcile intercompany payables
- [ ] Reconcile intercompany loans
- [ ] Verify intercompany eliminations

### Day 3: Adjusting Entries

#### Accruals
- [ ] Accrue unpaid expenses
- [ ] Accrue unpaid revenue
- [ ] Accrue payroll and benefits
- [ ] Accrue interest expense
- [ ] Accrue interest income

#### Prepaids
- [ ] Amortize prepaid expenses
- [ ] Amortize prepaid insurance
- [ ] Amortize prepaid rent
- [ ] Adjust other prepaid items

#### Depreciation
- [ ] Calculate depreciation expense
- [ ] Record depreciation journal entry
- [ ] Verify depreciation calculations
- [ ] Update fixed asset register

#### Amortization
- [ ] Calculate amortization expense
- [ ] Record amortization journal entry
- [ ] Verify amortization calculations

#### Other Adjustments
- [ ] Record bad debt expense (if applicable)
- [ ] Adjust inventory (if applicable)
- [ ] Record any other necessary adjustments
- [ ] Review all adjusting entries for accuracy

### Day 4: Financial Statement Preparation

#### Trial Balance
- [ ] Generate trial balance
- [ ] Review trial balance for errors
- [ ] Investigate and correct any errors
- [ ] Verify trial balance balances

#### Financial Statements
- [ ] Prepare Profit & Loss Statement
- [ ] Prepare Balance Sheet
- [ ] Prepare Cash Flow Statement (if required)
- [ ] Review financial statements for reasonableness
- [ ] Compare to prior month and budget
- [ ] Investigate significant variances

#### Supporting Schedules
- [ ] Prepare accounts receivable aging
- [ ] Prepare accounts payable aging
- [ ] Prepare inventory report (if applicable)
- [ ] Prepare fixed asset schedule
- [ ] Prepare other required schedules

### Day 5: Review and Finalization

#### Internal Review
- [ ] Review all journal entries
- [ ] Review all reconciliations
- [ ] Review financial statements
- [ ] Review supporting schedules
- [ ] Verify all accounts are reconciled
- [ ] Check for any missing transactions

#### Variance Analysis
- [ ] Compare actual to budget
- [ ] Compare to prior month
- [ ] Compare to prior year (if applicable)
- [ ] Document significant variances
- [ ] Prepare variance explanations

#### Management Review
- [ ] Present financial statements to management
- [ ] Review variances with management
- [ ] Address management questions
- [ ] Obtain management approval

#### Finalization
- [ ] Lock period in accounting system
- [ ] Print final financial statements
- [ ] File all closing documents
- [ ] Update financial dashboards
- [ ] Distribute financial statements

### Post-Close Tasks

#### Reporting
- [ ] Prepare management reports
- [ ] Prepare board reports (if applicable)
- [ ] Prepare investor reports (if applicable)
- [ ] Distribute reports to stakeholders

#### Tax Preparation
- [ ] Prepare estimated tax calculations (if required)
- [ ] Prepare sales tax returns (if required)
- [ ] Prepare other tax returns (if required)
- [ ] File tax returns by due dates

#### Documentation
- [ ] Complete closing checklist
- [ ] Document any issues or exceptions
- [ ] File closing documents
- [ ] Update closing procedures (if needed)

#### Follow-Up
- [ ] Address any open items from close
- [ ] Follow up on reconciling items
- [ ] Plan for next month's close
- [ ] Update closing procedures based on lessons learned

---

## Quality Standards

- **Timeliness**: Close completed within 5 business days of month-end
- **Accuracy**: All accounts reconciled and balanced
- **Completeness**: All transactions recorded and reviewed
- **Documentation**: All work documented and filed

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Issue: Trial Balance Doesn't Balance
- **Solution**: Review all journal entries, check for transposition errors, verify all accounts are included

### Issue: Bank Reconciliation Doesn't Balance
- **Solution**: Verify all transactions are recorded, check for timing differences, review prior month's reconciliation

### Issue: Significant Variances
- **Solution**: Investigate variances, document explanations, review with management

### Issue: Missing Transactions
- **Solution**: Review source documents, check with departments, record missing transactions
