# SOP: Customer Service & Support

## Purpose

This SOP defines the standards and processes for handling customer inquiries, support tickets, and complaints. It ensures a consistent, high-quality experience that fosters customer loyalty.

## Scope

Applies to all team members interacting with customers via email, phone, chat, or ticketing systems.

## Responsibilities

-   **Support Agent**: First-line response and resolution.
-   **Support Manager**: Escalations and quality review.
-   **Product/Service Team**: Technical resolution for complex issues.

## Process Steps

### 1. Ticket Receipt & Triage
-   [ ] **Acknowledge**: Automated receipt email sent immediately.
-   [ ] **Categorize**: Tag ticket by topic (e.g., Billing, Technical, General).
-   [ ] **Prioritize**: Assign priority level (Low, Medium, High, Critical).

### 2. Response
-   **Standard Response Time**: Within [NUMBER] hours.
-   [ ] **Review**: Read customer history and context.
-   [ ] **Respond**: Use templates for common issues but personalize the opening/closing.
-   [ ] **Solve**: Provide clear, step-by-step instructions or resolution.

### 3. Escalation (If needed)
-   **Trigger**: Issue cannot be resolved within [TIME] or requires higher authority.
-   [ ] **Transfer**: Assign to Level 2 Support or Manager.
-   [ ] **Inform**: Notify customer of escalation and expected timeline.

### 4. Resolution & Closure
-   [ ] **Confirm**: Ask customer if the issue is resolved.
-   [ ] **Close**: Mark ticket as "Resolved".
-   [ ] **Feedback**: Send CSAT (Customer Satisfaction) survey.

## Quality Standards

-   **First Response Time (FRT)**: < [NUMBER] hours.
-   **Resolution Time**: < [NUMBER] hours.
-   **CSAT Score**: > 4.5/5.0.
-   **Tone**: Empathetic, professional, and solution-oriented.

## Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Definition | Response Time | Who to Notify |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical** | System down, data loss | 15 mins | CTO/Founder |
| **High** | Feature broken, billing error | 2 hours | Support Manager |
| **Medium** | How-to question, minor bug | 24 hours | Support Agent |
| **Low** | Feature request, feedback | 48 hours | Support Agent |
