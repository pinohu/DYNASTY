# SOP: Service Delivery Process

## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the end-to-end process for delivering services to clients. It ensures consistency, quality, and client satisfaction across all engagements.

## Scope

Applies to all service-based deliverables within [COMPANY NAME].

## Responsibilities

-   **Account Manager**: Client communication and expectations.
-   **Project Manager**: Timeline and resource management.
-   **Service Team**: Execution of deliverables.
-   **QA Specialist**: Review and approval.

## Process Steps

### 1. Project Initiation
-   [ ] Review signed contract and scope of work.
-   [ ] Conduct internal kickoff meeting.
-   [ ] Assign team members and resources.
-   [ ] Set up project in project management tool (e.g., Asana/ClickUp).

### 2. Execution
-   [ ] Execute tasks according to project plan.
-   [ ] Maintain regular communication with client (weekly updates).
-   [ ] Track time and expenses.
-   [ ] Identify and flag risks immediately.

### 3. Quality Assurance (QA)
-   [ ] Peer review of all deliverables.
-   [ ] Verify against scope of work requirements.
-   [ ] Correct any errors or omissions.

### 4. Delivery
-   [ ] Present final deliverables to client.
-   [ ] Obtain written acceptance/sign-off.
-   [ ] Address any final feedback.

### 5. Closure
-   [ ] Send final invoice.
-   [ ] Archive project files.
-   [ ] Conduct internal retrospective (lessons learned).
-   [ ] Request client testimonial/referral.

## Quality Standards

-   **Timeliness**: 100% of milestones met on or before deadline.
-   **Accuracy**: Zero critical errors in final deliverables.
-   **Communication**: Response time < 24 hours.
