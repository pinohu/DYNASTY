# Client Onboarding Process Map

## Purpose

This process map provides a visual and detailed breakdown of the client onboarding process. It shows the flow from contract signing through successful service delivery.

## Process Overview

```
Contract Signed → Welcome → Information Collection → Kickoff → Setup → Training → First Delivery → Check-In → Complete
```

## Detailed Process Steps

### Stage 1: Contract Execution (Day 0)

**Input**: Signed service agreement

**Activities**:
1. Receive signed contract
2. Verify all signatures and dates
3. File contract in client file
4. Create client record in CRM
5. Assign account manager

**Output**: Client record created, contract filed

**Owner**: Operations Manager

**Duration**: Same day

---

### Stage 2: Welcome & Initial Contact (Day 0-1)

**Input**: Client record created

**Activities**:
1. Send welcome email (within 24 hours)
2. Send welcome package (if applicable)
3. Schedule kickoff call
4. Assign client success manager

**Output**: Welcome email sent, kickoff scheduled

**Owner**: Client Success Manager

**Duration**: 1-2 days

---

### Stage 3: Information Collection (Day 1-3)

**Input**: Welcome email sent

**Activities**:
1. Request required documents
2. Collect client contact information
3. Gather service-specific requirements
4. Set up client portal access
5. Complete compliance checks

**Output**: All required information collected

**Owner**: Administrative Staff

**Duration**: 2-3 days

---

### Stage 4: Kickoff Meeting (Day 3-5)

**Input**: Information collected

**Activities**:
1. Conduct kickoff call/meeting
2. Review service agreement and scope
3. Discuss goals and expectations
4. Identify key stakeholders
5. Set up communication protocols
6. Document meeting notes

**Output**: Kickoff completed, expectations aligned

**Owner**: Account Manager / Client Success Manager

**Duration**: 1-2 hours (meeting) + 1 day (follow-up)

---

### Stage 5: Service Setup (Day 5-7)

**Input**: Kickoff completed

**Activities**:
1. Set up service-specific systems
2. Configure client portal
3. Create project/service records
4. Assign service delivery team
5. Test all systems and access

**Output**: Systems configured, ready for service delivery

**Owner**: Operations Team

**Duration**: 2-3 days

---

### Stage 6: Training & Orientation (Day 7-10)

**Input**: Systems set up

**Activities**:
1. Conduct client portal training (if applicable)
2. Train client on service processes
3. Introduce service delivery team
4. Provide process documentation
5. Answer questions

**Output**: Client trained and oriented

**Owner**: Client Success Manager

**Duration**: 2-3 days

---

### Stage 7: First Service Delivery (Day 7-14)

**Input**: Training completed

**Activities**:
1. Execute first service delivery
2. Follow service-specific procedures
3. Ensure quality standards met
4. Document service delivery
5. Collect client feedback

**Output**: First service delivered, client feedback collected

**Owner**: Service Delivery Team

**Duration**: Varies by service type

---

### Stage 8: Check-In & Optimization (Day 14-21)

**Input**: First service delivered

**Activities**:
1. Conduct check-in call/meeting
2. Review first service experience
3. Address questions or concerns
4. Make adjustments if needed
5. Confirm satisfaction

**Output**: Client satisfied, any issues resolved

**Owner**: Client Success Manager

**Duration**: 1 week

---

### Stage 9: Onboarding Complete (Day 21-30)

**Input**: Check-in completed

**Activities**:
1. Conduct onboarding review
2. Confirm all steps completed
3. Transition to ongoing management
4. Send onboarding survey
5. Update client status in CRM

**Output**: Onboarding complete, client in active status

**Owner**: Client Success Manager

**Duration**: 1 week

---

## Process Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Time to Kickoff | < 5 days | Days from contract to kickoff |
| Time to First Delivery | < 14 days | Days from contract to first service |
| Onboarding Completion Time | < 30 days | Days from contract to onboarding complete |
| Client Satisfaction (Onboarding) | > 4.5/5.0 | Survey score |
| Information Collection Rate | 100% | % of required info collected |

---

## Decision Points

### Decision 1: Qualification Check
- **If Qualified**: Proceed to Stage 2
- **If Not Qualified**: Escalate to management

### Decision 2: Information Complete?
- **If Yes**: Proceed to Stage 4
- **If No**: Follow up, extend deadline if needed

### Decision 3: Client Satisfied with First Service?
- **If Yes**: Proceed to Stage 8
- **If No**: Address concerns, make adjustments
