# SubTo Due Diligence Checklist

## Purpose
To verify the viability and safety of a Subject-To real estate acquisition before closing.

## Loan Verification

- [ ] **Mortgage Statement**: Obtained most recent statement.
- [ ] **Loan Type**: Verified (Fixed vs. ARM).
- [ ] **Interest Rate**: Verified rate is acceptable.
- [ ] **Monthly Payment (PITI)**: Verified Principal, Interest, Taxes, Insurance.
- [ ] **Arrears**: Confirmed amount to reinstate (if any).
- [ ] **Balloon Payment**: Confirmed no upcoming balloon.
- [ ] **Prepayment Penalty**: Confirmed no penalty for early payoff.
- [ ] **Loan Status**: Confirmed not in active foreclosure (or have time to stop it).

## Title & Liens

- [ ] **Title Search**: Ordered and reviewed.
- [ ] **Judgments**: Checked for judgments against seller.
- [ ] **Tax Liens**: Checked for unpaid property taxes.
- [ ] **HOA Liens**: Checked for unpaid HOA dues.
- [ ] **Solar/Utility Liens**: Checked for PACE loans or solar leases.
- [ ] **Second Mortgages**: Confirmed no unclosed HELOCs or 2nd liens.

## Property Condition

- [ ] **Inspection**: Physical inspection completed.
- [ ] **Major Systems**: Roof, HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical checked.
- [ ] **Foundation**: Checked for issues.
- [ ] **Occupancy**: Verified if vacant or tenant-occupied.
    - [ ] If occupied: Lease agreement reviewed.
    - [ ] Estoppel certificate signed by tenant.

## Compliance & Risk

- [ ] **HOA Rules**: Reviewed for rental restrictions.
- [ ] **Insurance**: Quote obtained for "Landlord Policy" naming Trust as insured.
- [ ] **Due-on-Sale**: Risk assessed; reserves available.
- [ ] **Seller Situation**: Verified seller's motivation and understanding.
    - [ ] "CYA" Disclosure signed.

## Financial Projections

- [ ] **Entry Fee**: Cash to Seller + Arrears + Closing Costs + Reno.
- [ ] **Market Rent**: Verified via Rentometer/Zillow.
- [ ] **Cash Flow**: (Rent - PITI - Reserves) > $300/mo?
- [ ] **Exit Strategy**: Defined (Long term, Mid term, Wrap).
