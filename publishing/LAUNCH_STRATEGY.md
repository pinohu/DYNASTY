# The Grand Slam Launch Strategy

**Goal:** Achieve "Bestseller" status in at least 3 Amazon categories within 48 hours of launch.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1: The "Whisper" Campaign
- **Action:** Send a text-only email to your existing list (Clients, Friends, Family).
- **Script:** "I'm writing a book. Actually, I'm writing four. It's everything I know about building the empire. Reply 'LEGACY' if you want to be on the Early Reader team."
- **Goal:** Get 50-100 "Early Readers" who commit to reviewing the book on Day 1.

### Week 2: Cover Reveal & Asset Prep
- **Action:** Post the cover art options on LinkedIn/Facebook/Twitter. "A or B? Help me choose."
- **Goal:** Engagement and social proof.
- **Task:** Finalize KDP upload (Pre-order setup).

### Week 3: The "Value Drop"
- **Action:** Release "Chapter 1" of Book 1 for free as a PDF lead magnet.
- **Channel:** LinkedIn Article + Email Blast.
- **Call to Action:** "Pre-order the full set for $0.99 (Limited Time)."

### Week 4: The Review Team Activation
- **Action:** Send the digital manuscripts (ARC - Advanced Reader Copies) to the Week 1 "Early Readers".
- **Requirement:** "Read Chapter 1 and 2. Be ready to post a review on [Launch Date]."

---

## Phase 2: Launch Week (The 5-Day Sprint)

### Day 1: The Soft Launch (Price: $0.99)
- **Target:** Early Reader Team ONLY.
- **Action:** Email: "It's live. Go get it. Reply 'DONE' when you've posted your review."
- **Goal:** 20 Verified Reviews in 24 hours.

### Day 2: The Public Blast
- **Target:** Entire Email List + Social Media.
- **Action:** "The Legacy Codex is live. For the next 24 hours, it's $0.99."
- **Goal:** Velocity (Sales per hour ranking).

### Day 3: The "Best Seller" Screenshot
- **Action:** Take a screenshot of the Amazon Category Ranking (Top 10, Top 1, etc.).
- **Post:** "We just hit #1 in Estate Planning. Thank you! If you haven't grabbed it, price goes up tomorrow."

### Day 4: The Price Pivot
- **Action:** Raise price to $4.99 or $9.99.
- **Messaging:** "The Launch Special is ending. Last chance."

### Day 5: The "Thank You" & Upsell
- **Action:** Email: "Thank you for the support. If you bought the book, you qualify for a free 'Legacy Strategy Session' (Valued at $500)."
- **Goal:** Convert book buyers into High-Ticket Consulting Clients.

---

## Phase 3: The Evergreen Machine

1.  **Paperback/Hardcover:** Enable "Print on Demand" 2 weeks after eBook launch.
2.  **Audiobook:** Record "Book 1" yourself. It builds massive trust.
3.  **Funnel:** Add a QR code in the first chapter of every book leading to the `dashboard/intake` page.
    *   *Reader -> QR Code -> Intake Form -> High Ticket Client.*
