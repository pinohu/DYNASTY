### 7.3 TAX OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

**ENTITY-LEVEL TAX STRATEGIES**

**S-Corporation Election (CXI LLC)**

Mechanism:
- CXI LLC elects S-Corporation taxation (Form 2553 filed)
- Generates payroll for Primary & Secondary Principal
- Pays reasonable salary: $60,000-$80,000
- Remaining profits distributed as S-Corp distributions (no FICA tax)

Tax Savings Example:
- Annual S-Corp profits: $120,000
- Salary portion: $70,000 (subject to FICA: $10,710)
- Distribution portion: $50,000 (no FICA tax)
- **Annual FICA savings**: $7,650 (on $50K distribution)
- **5-year savings**: $38,250

**Real Estate Professional Status (REPS)**

Qualification:
- Material participation: 750+ hours annually
- >50% of work time in real estate
- Valid grouping election (if multi-state)

Tax Benefits:
1. Unlimited real estate loss deductions
   - Real estate losses offset W-2 income
   - Example: $50K loss offsets $50K salary (no tax on $50K)
   
2. Bonus depreciation acceleration
   - 100% first-year depreciation on new equipment
   - Reduced depreciation recapture on sale
   - Accelerated cost recovery
   
3. Cost segregation studies
   - Break property into components
   - Accelerate depreciation on some components
   - $100K property might depreciate $15K+ year 1
   - Example: $750K property × $15K/100K = $112,500 year 1 depreciation

**Annual Tax Savings Scenario**:
- W-2 income: $70,000
- Real estate losses (REPS): -$50,000
- Taxable income: $20,000
- Tax saved: $50,000 × 37% = $18,500 (top rate)
- Plus depreciation deduction (bonus): $25,000 × 37% = $9,250
- **Total annual tax savings**: $27,750 (conservative)

**PERSONAL TAX STRATEGIES**

**Children Employment Strategy**

Mechanism:
- Each child employed by family business
- Wages up to $13,850/year (standard deduction for dependent)
- Additional $6,500 to Roth IRA (self-directed investment)
- All tax-free (no income tax or FICA tax under age 18)

Family Benefits:
- Child develops work ethic and business skills
- Child builds Roth IRA for retirement
- Child receives legitimate business income (deductible)
- Family reduces taxable income

Tax Savings Example (4 children):
- Each child: $13,850/year (reasonable business compensation)
- Family total: $55,400
- Tax rate (25%): $13,850
- **Annual family tax savings**: $13,850+
- Plus Roth IRA contributions: $26,000/year (4 × $6,500)

**Augusta Rule (Home Office Rental)**

Mechanism:
- Business rents home for 14 days/year
