# Client Update Email Template

## Purpose

This template is for sending regular updates to existing clients about project progress, service changes, or important announcements. It maintains engagement and demonstrates proactive communication.

## Instructions

1. **Customize**: Replace all placeholders with actual information
2. **Be Specific**: Include concrete details, not vague statements
3. **Add Value**: Each email should provide value, not just check a box
4. **Timing**: Send updates at appropriate intervals (weekly, monthly, quarterly)
5. **Track**: Monitor opens and engagement

## Template

```markdown
Subject: [Project Name] Update - [Key Milestone or Topic]

---

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

I wanted to give you a quick update on [PROJECT/SERVICE NAME].

**What We've Accomplished**

Since our last update, we've:

- [ ] [ACCOMPLISHMENT 1]
- [ ] [ACCOMPLISHMENT 2]
- [ ] [ACCOMPLISHMENT 3]

**What's Next**

Over the next [TIME PERIOD], we'll be focusing on:

- [NEXT STEP 1]
- [NEXT STEP 2]
- [NEXT STEP 3]

**Timeline**

We're on track to complete [MILESTONE] by [DATE]. [ADD ANY TIMELINE NOTES OR ADJUSTMENTS]

**Questions or Concerns?**

If you have any questions or need clarification on anything, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm here to help!

**Access Your Project**

You can always view the latest updates in your client portal: [LINK]

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR TITLE]
[COMPANY NAME]
[PHONE]
[EMAIL]
```

## Best Practices

- **Frequency**: Don't over-communicate - weekly or bi-weekly is usually sufficient
- **Value**: Each update should provide value, not just status
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly
- **Length**: Keep it concise - busy clients appreciate brevity
- **Visuals**: Consider including screenshots, charts, or progress indicators
