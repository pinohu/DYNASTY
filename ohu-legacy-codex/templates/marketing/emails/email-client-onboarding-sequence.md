# Client Onboarding Email Sequence Template

## Purpose

This template provides a complete email sequence for onboarding new clients. The sequence guides clients through the onboarding process, sets expectations, and ensures a smooth transition to active service delivery.

## Instructions

1. **Customize**: Replace all [PLACEHOLDERS] with actual information
2. **Schedule**: Set up automated sequence or send manually
3. **Personalize**: Add specific details about client's service
4. **Time**: Send emails at appropriate intervals (see timing below)
5. **Track**: Monitor opens, clicks, and responses
6. **Adjust**: Modify sequence based on client feedback

## Email Sequence

## Sequence Summary

| Email | Timing | Purpose | Key Action |
|-------|--------|---------|------------|
| Welcome | Day 0 | Welcome and introduction | Set expectations |
| Preparation | Day 1 | Prepare for kickoff | Gather information |
| Post-Kickoff | Day 2 | Follow up on call | Confirm next steps |
| Account Ready | Day 3-5 | Account setup complete | Access portal |
| First Delivery | Day 7-14 | First service delivered | Review deliverables |
| Check-In | Day 14-21 | Gather feedback | Collect feedback |
| Complete | Day 30 | Onboarding complete | Transition to ongoing |

## Best Practices

- **Personalization**: Customize each email with client-specific information
- **Timing**: Send emails at appropriate intervals (not too frequent, not too sparse)
- **Value**: Each email should provide value, not just check boxes
- **Responsiveness**: Respond promptly to client replies
- **Tracking**: Monitor email opens, clicks, and responses
- **Adjustment**: Modify sequence based on client feedback and results

## Variations

### Short Sequence (Simple Services)
- Welcome Email
- Account Ready Email
- First Delivery Email

### Extended Sequence (Complex Services)
- Add additional check-in emails
- Add training emails
- Add resource emails
