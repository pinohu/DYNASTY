# Social Media Post Template

## Purpose

This template provides a framework for creating engaging social media posts across platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X). It ensures consistency and maximizes engagement.

## Instructions

1. **Choose Platform**: Adapt format for specific platform
2. **Craft Hook**: First line must grab attention
3. **Add Value**: Provide useful information or insight
4. **Include CTA**: Tell audience what to do next
5. **Use Hashtags**: Include relevant hashtags (platform-dependent)

## Template Structure

### LinkedIn Post

**Format**: Professional, value-driven, longer form (1,300-3,000 characters)

```markdown
[HOOK - First line that grabs attention]

[VALUE - Share insight, tip, or story]

[ENGAGEMENT - Ask question or invite discussion]

[CTA - Link to article, website, or next step]

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3
```

---

### Facebook Post

**Format**: Conversational, engaging, 40-80 characters for best engagement

```markdown
[HOOK]

[STORY OR VALUE]

[QUESTION TO ENGAGE]

[CTA]

[LINK]
```

---

### Instagram Post

**Format**: Visual-first, concise caption, use emojis sparingly

**Caption**:
```markdown
[HOOK]

[VALUE OR STORY]

[CTA]

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3 #Hashtag4 #Hashtag5
```

**Note**: Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags, but 5-10 is optimal.

---

### Twitter/X Post

**Format**: Concise, punchy, under 280 characters

```markdown
[HOOK]

[VALUE OR INSIGHT]

[CTA OR QUESTION]

[LINK]
```

## Post Variations

### Variation 1: Educational Post

**Hook**: "Did you know that [STATISTIC OR FACT]?"

**Body**: "[EXPLANATION OR INSIGHT]"

**CTA**: "What's your experience with this? Share in the comments."

---

### Variation 2: Story Post

**Hook**: "Last week, I [PERSONAL STORY]..."

**Body**: "[LESSON LEARNED OR INSIGHT]"

**CTA**: "Have you had a similar experience?"

---

### Variation 3: Question Post

**Hook**: "What's the biggest challenge you face with [TOPIC]?"

**Body**: "[CONTEXT OR YOUR PERSPECTIVE]"

**CTA**: "Share your thoughts below - I read every comment!"

---

### Variation 4: Value Tip Post

**Hook**: "Here's a quick tip that will [BENEFIT]..."

**Body**: "[TIP OR ADVICE]"

**CTA**: "Save this post for later and follow for more tips!"

---

### Variation 5: Behind-the-Scenes Post

**Hook**: "Ever wonder how we [DO SOMETHING]?"

**Body**: "[BEHIND-THE-SCENES CONTENT]"

**CTA**: "Want to see more? Follow us for daily insights!"

## Best Practices

### Content
- **Value First**: Every post should provide value
- **Authentic**: Be genuine and human
- **Consistent**: Maintain brand voice
- **Visual**: Include images or videos when possible

### Timing
- **LinkedIn**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM
- **Facebook**: Tuesday-Thursday, 1-3 PM
- **Instagram**: Tuesday-Friday, 11 AM-1 PM or 7-9 PM
- **Twitter**: Monday-Friday, 8-9 AM or 12-1 PM

### Engagement
- **Respond**: Reply to comments within 24 hours
- **Ask Questions**: Encourage discussion
- **Tag Relevant**: Tag partners, clients, or industry leaders (when appropriate)
- **Share Others' Content**: Build relationships through sharing

### Hashtags
- **LinkedIn**: 3-5 hashtags
- **Facebook**: 1-2 hashtags
- **Instagram**: 5-10 hashtags
- **Twitter**: 1-2 hashtags
