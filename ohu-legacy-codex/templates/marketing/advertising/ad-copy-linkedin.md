# LinkedIn Ads Copy Template

## Purpose

This template provides frameworks for writing effective LinkedIn ad copy for B2B marketing. LinkedIn ads work best with professional, value-driven messaging.

## Instructions

1. **Target Audience**: Write for professional decision-makers
2. **Professional Tone**: Maintain business-appropriate language
3. **Value-Focused**: Emphasize ROI and business outcomes
4. **Clear CTA**: Professional but direct call-to-action
5. **Test Variations**: Create multiple versions for optimization

## Template Structure

### LinkedIn Sponsored Content

**Headline** (150 characters):
> [BENEFIT OR VALUE PROPOSITION FOR TARGET AUDIENCE]

**Description** (600 characters):
> [EXPAND ON HEADLINE]
> 
> [PROOF POINT OR CASE STUDY]
> 
> [CLEAR CTA]

**CTA Button**: [Learn More / Download / Register / Get Started]

## Best Practices

### Targeting
- **Job Title**: Target specific roles (CEO, CFO, Marketing Director)
- **Company Size**: Match to your ideal client size
- **Industry**: Focus on relevant industries
- **Seniority**: Target decision-makers

### Messaging
- **Professional**: Maintain business tone
- **Data-Driven**: Use statistics and proof points
- **ROI-Focused**: Emphasize business outcomes
- **Respectful**: Don't be overly salesy

### CTAs
- **Clear**: "Download," "Register," "Learn More"
- **Value-Driven**: "Get Free Guide," "See Case Study"
- **Action-Oriented**: Use action verbs
