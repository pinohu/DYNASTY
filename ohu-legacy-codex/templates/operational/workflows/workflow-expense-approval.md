# Expense Approval Workflow

## Purpose

This workflow defines the process for employees to request and receive approval for business expenses. It ensures budget control and proper documentation for accounting.

## Scope

Applies to all employees and contractors incurring costs on behalf of the company.

## Limits

-   **Pre-Approval Required**: Expenses over $[AMOUNT].
-   **Auto-Approval**: Recurring subscriptions under $[AMOUNT].

## Workflow Steps

### 1. Request (Pre-Spend)
*Required for expenses over $[AMOUNT]*
1.  Employee submits request via [EMAIL/SLACK/FORM].
2.  Request includes: Item description, Cost, Business Purpose, Budget Category.
3.  Manager reviews against budget.
4.  **Decision**:
    -   **Approved**: Employee proceeds.
    -   **Denied**: Employee notified with reason.

### 2. Purchase
1.  Employee makes purchase using [COMPANY CARD / PERSONAL CARD].
2.  Employee **MUST** save the itemized receipt.

### 3. Submission (Post-Spend)
1.  Employee logs expense in [EXPENSE SOFTWARE] (e.g., Expensify, QuickBooks).
2.  Employee attaches receipt image.
3.  Employee categorizes expense (e.g., Travel, Meals, Software).
4.  Deadline: Submit by the [DAY] of the month.

### 4. Review & Reimbursement
1.  Finance Manager reviews report for:
    -   Receipt match.
    -   Policy compliance.
    -   Correct categorization.
2.  **Approval**: Report approved for payment.
3.  **Reimbursement**: Funds transferred to employee via [PAYROLL/ACH] within [NUMBER] days.

## Policy Highlights

-   **Meals**: Limit of $[AMOUNT] per person.
-   **Travel**: Economy class only; book [NUMBER] days in advance.
-   **Receipts**: Required for ALL expenses over $[AMOUNT].
