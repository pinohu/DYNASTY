# Daily Operations Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist ensures that critical daily tasks are completed to maintain smooth business operations. It covers administrative, financial, and service-related activities.

## Instructions

1.  **Assign**: Assign checklist to the Office Manager or Operations Assistant.
2.  **Execute**: Complete tasks daily.
3.  **Review**: Operations Manager reviews weekly for compliance.

## Checklist

### Morning Routine (Start of Day)
- [ ] **Check Communications**: Review general email inbox and voicemail.
- [ ] **System Check**: Verify all software systems (CRM, PM, etc.) are operational.
- [ ] **Calendar Review**: Confirm daily appointments and team availability.
- [ ] **Office Prep**: Ensure office supplies/equipment are ready (if physical).

### Financial Tasks
- [ ] **Bank Feed**: Review daily bank transactions for anomalies.
- [ ] **Invoicing**: Send any immediate invoices or receipts.
- [ ] **Expenses**: Log daily receipts/expenses.

### Service/Operations
- [ ] **Ticket Review**: Check support tickets/client requests.
- [ ] **Project Status**: Update status of active projects.
- [ ] **Team Sync**: Conduct daily stand-up (15 mins).

### End of Day Routine
- [ ] **Clean Up**: Organize digital and physical workspace.
- [ ] **Security**: Ensure sensitive data is secured/locked.
- [ ] **Prep for Tomorrow**: Review next day's priority list.
- [ ] **Time Logs**: Verify all team time logs are submitted.
