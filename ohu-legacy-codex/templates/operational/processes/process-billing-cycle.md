# Billing Cycle Process Map

## Purpose

This process map outlines the complete billing cycle from invoice generation through payment collection and reconciliation. It ensures consistent cash flow and proper accounting.

## Process Overview

```
Service Delivery → Invoice Generation → Invoice Delivery → Payment Monitoring → Payment Application → Reconciliation → Collections (if needed)
```

## Detailed Process Steps

### Stage 1: Service Delivery Documentation (Ongoing)

**Input**: Services provided

**Activities**:
1. Track billable hours/milestones
2. Document services delivered
3. Record expenses to be reimbursed
4. Update project/service records
5. Verify against contract/agreement

**Output**: Service delivery documented

**Owner**: Service Delivery Team / Project Manager

**Duration**: Ongoing

---

### Stage 2: Invoice Generation (Monthly/Per Project)

**Input**: Service delivery documented

**Activities**:
1. Review billable items for period
2. Verify against contract terms
3. Calculate invoice amounts
4. Generate invoice in accounting system
5. Review invoice for accuracy
6. Attach supporting documentation (if required)

**Output**: Invoice generated and ready

**Owner**: Billing Specialist / Finance Manager

**Duration**: 1-2 days

**Timing**: [SPECIFY - e.g., "By 5th of month for prior month services"]

---

### Stage 3: Invoice Delivery (Day 1)

**Input**: Invoice generated

**Activities**:
1. Send invoice via email to billing contact
2. Attach detailed time logs/receipts (if required)
3. Send copy to account manager (if applicable)
4. Confirm receipt (if automated system)
5. Log invoice in tracking system

**Output**: Invoice delivered to client

**Owner**: Billing Specialist

**Duration**: Same day

---

### Stage 4: Payment Monitoring (Days 1-30)

**Input**: Invoice delivered

**Activities**:
1. Monitor bank feed for incoming payments
2. Check payment tracking system daily
3. Match payments to invoices
4. Identify partial payments
5. Flag overdue invoices

**Output**: Payment status tracked

**Owner**: Finance Manager / Billing Specialist

**Duration**: Ongoing until paid

---

### Stage 5: Payment Application (Upon Receipt)

**Input**: Payment received

**Activities**:
1. Identify which invoice(s) payment applies to
2. Apply payment in accounting system
3. Update invoice status to "Paid"
4. Send payment confirmation to client (if applicable)
5. Update cash flow records

**Output**: Payment applied, invoice closed

**Owner**: Finance Manager

**Duration**: Same day as receipt

---

### Stage 6: Reconciliation (Monthly)

**Input**: Payments applied

**Activities**:
1. Reconcile accounts receivable
2. Verify all payments applied correctly
3. Identify any discrepancies
4. Resolve unmatched payments
5. Generate AR aging report

**Output**: AR reconciled

**Owner**: Finance Manager

**Duration**: 1-2 days (monthly)

---

### Stage 7: Collections (If Overdue)

**Input**: Invoice past due

**Activities**:

**3 Days Past Due**:
1. Send automated reminder email
2. Update invoice status

**15 Days Past Due**:
1. Send personal email from Billing Specialist
2. Call client billing contact
3. Document communication

**30 Days Past Due**:
1. Phone call from Account Manager
2. Discuss payment plan if needed
3. Escalate to management

**60 Days Past Due**:
1. Send formal demand letter
2. Consider service suspension (if contract allows)
3. Legal review if necessary

**90+ Days Past Due**:
1. Legal action or collections agency
2. Write off as bad debt (if appropriate)

**Output**: Payment collected or escalated

**Owner**: Billing Specialist / Account Manager / Management

**Duration**: Varies

---

## Process Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Invoice Accuracy | 100% | % of invoices with no errors |
| Invoice Delivery Time | < 3 days | Days from period end to invoice sent |
| Payment Terms Compliance | > 90% | % of invoices paid on time |
| Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) | < 45 days | Average days to collect payment |
| Collection Rate | > 95% | % of invoices eventually collected |

---

## Decision Points

### Decision 1: Invoice Ready?
- **If Yes**: Proceed to delivery
- **If No**: Resolve issues, then deliver

### Decision 2: Payment Received?
- **If Yes**: Apply payment
- **If No**: Continue monitoring

### Decision 3: Invoice Overdue?
- **If Yes**: Initiate collections process
- **If No**: Continue monitoring
