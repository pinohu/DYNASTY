# Tenant Screening Criteria Template

## Purpose
To establish objective, non-discriminatory standards for selecting tenants. This document should be provided to applicants before they apply.

## General Requirements

1.  **Application**: Every occupant 18 years or older must complete an application.
2.  **Fee**: Non-refundable application fee of $_____ per applicant.
3.  **ID**: Valid government-issued photo ID required.

## Income Requirements

1.  **Gross Income**: Combined household income must be at least **3x** the monthly rent.
2.  **Verification**:
    -   Employed: Last 2 pay stubs.
    -   Self-Employed: Last 2 years tax returns + 3 months bank statements.
    -   New Job: Offer letter on company letterhead.

## Credit Requirements

1.  **Credit Score**: Minimum FICO score of **600**.
2.  **History**:
    -   No open bankruptcies.
    -   No judgments from landlords or utility companies.
    -   Medical/Student debt is excluded from consideration.

## Rental History

1.  **Verification**: We verify the last 2 years of rental history.
2.  **Standing**:
    -   No evictions in the last 5 years.
    -   No broken leases or unpaid balances to landlords.
    -   Positive reference from previous landlord (paid on time, cared for property).

## Criminal History

1.  **Screening**: A criminal background check will be conducted.
2.  **Disqualifiers**:
    -   Convictions for violent crimes, drug manufacturing/distribution, or sex offenses may be grounds for denial, subject to individual assessment and local laws.

2.  **Restrictions**: No aggressive breeds (Pit Bull, Rottweiler, Doberman, etc.) due to insurance.
3.  **Fees**:
    -   Pet Deposit: $_____ (Refundable/Non-Refundable)
    -   Pet Rent: $_____ / month

## Occupancy

1.  **Limit**: Maximum of 2 persons per bedroom + 1.
