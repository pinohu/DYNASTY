# Monthly Cash Flow Statement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a comprehensive monthly Cash Flow Statement for tracking cash inflows and outflows. Use this template to monitor cash position, identify cash flow trends, and plan for cash needs.

## Instructions

1. **Select Period**: Enter the month and year for the statement
2. **Starting Cash**: Enter beginning cash balance
3. **Operating Activities**: Record cash from operations
4. **Investing Activities**: Record cash from investing activities
5. **Financing Activities**: Record cash from financing activities
6. **Calculate Ending Cash**: Sum all activities to get ending cash balance
7. **Reconcile**: Reconcile to bank statements
8. **Analyze**: Review cash flow trends and variances

## Notes

- **Timing**: Prepare monthly cash flow statement within 5-10 business days after month-end

- **Reconciliation**: Always reconcile to bank statements to ensure accuracy

- **Operating Focus**: Positive operating cash flow is critical for business sustainability

- **Trend Analysis**: Compare to prior periods to identify trends and potential issues

- **Forecasting**: Use historical cash flow data to forecast future cash needs

- **Working Capital**: Monitor changes in working capital as they impact cash flow
