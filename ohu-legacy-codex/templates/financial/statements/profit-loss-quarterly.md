# Quarterly Profit & Loss Statement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a quarterly view of the Profit & Loss statement. It aggregates monthly data to show performance over a three-month period and year-to-date (YTD).

## Instructions

1.  **Aggregate Data**: Sum revenue and expenses for the three months of the quarter.
2.  **Calculate YTD**: Add the current quarter to previous quarters for YTD totals.
3.  **Analyze Trends**: Compare Q1 vs Q2, etc., to identify seasonal trends.
