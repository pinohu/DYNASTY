# Monthly Profit & Loss Statement Template

## Purpose

This template provides a comprehensive monthly Profit & Loss (P&L) statement for tracking business performance. Use this template to monitor revenue, expenses, and profitability on a monthly basis.

## Instructions

1. **Select Period**: Enter the month and year for the statement
2. **Enter Revenue**: Record all revenue sources for the month
3. **Enter Expenses**: Record all expenses by category
4. **Calculate Totals**: Sum revenue and expenses
5. **Calculate Net Income**: Subtract total expenses from total revenue
6. **Compare to Budget**: Compare actual results to budgeted amounts
7. **Review Variances**: Analyze significant variances from budget
8. **Distribute**: Share with management and stakeholders
