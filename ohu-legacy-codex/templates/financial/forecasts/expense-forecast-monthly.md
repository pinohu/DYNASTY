# Monthly Expense Forecast Template

## Purpose

This template helps project future expenses. It separates fixed costs (predictable) from variable costs (tied to revenue) to provide a clear picture of future spending.

## Instructions

1.  **Fixed Costs**: Enter recurring monthly costs (Rent, Salaries).
2.  **Variable Costs**: Estimate costs as a percentage of revenue or per unit.
3.  **One-Time Costs**: Include planned large purchases or annual fees.
