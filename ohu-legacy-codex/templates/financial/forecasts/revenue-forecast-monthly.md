# Monthly Revenue Forecast Template

## Purpose

This template focuses specifically on projecting revenue. It allows for detailed breakdowns by product line, service, or customer segment to build a bottom-up revenue projection.

## Instructions

1.  **Segments**: Break down revenue by key drivers (e.g., Product A, Service B).
2.  **Drivers**: Estimate volume (units/hours) and price.
3.  **Seasonality**: Adjust for seasonal trends.
