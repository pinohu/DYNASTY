# Entity Tax Comparison Worksheet

## Purpose

This template helps compare tax implications of different entity structures (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp) to determine the most tax-efficient structure for your business.

**IMPORTANT**: Tax laws change frequently. Consult with a CPA or tax attorney for current-year analysis.

## Instructions

1. **Enter Income**: Project annual business income
2. **Enter Expenses**: Project annual business expenses
3. **Calculate for Each Entity**: Compute taxes for LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp
4. **Compare Results**: Identify most tax-efficient structure
5. **Consider Non-Tax Factors**: Liability, compliance, flexibility
