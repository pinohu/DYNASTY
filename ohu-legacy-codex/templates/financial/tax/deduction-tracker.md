# Business Deduction Tracker

## Purpose

This template helps track business expenses throughout the year to maximize tax deductions. Use this to organize receipts and ensure no deductions are missed.

## Instructions

1. **Record Expenses**: Enter expenses as they occur
2. **Categorize**: Use appropriate tax categories
3. **Attach Receipts**: Link to digital receipts or note location
4. **Review Monthly**: Review monthly to ensure completeness
5. **Year-End Summary**: Summarize for tax preparation
