**90-100 (Green Zone)**: Excellent - Empire is thriving
- Continue current strategies
- Look for expansion opportunities
- Maintain momentum

- Address any yellow zone items
- Strengthen weak areas
- Prevent decline to orange

**60-74 (Orange Zone)**: Fair - Take corrective action
- Immediate attention required
- Develop improvement plans
- Allocate resources to critical areas

**<60 (Red Zone)**: Poor - Emergency intervention required
- Activate emergency protocols if needed
- Family Council emergency meeting
- Consider "Fortress Mode" (see Empire Constitution)
