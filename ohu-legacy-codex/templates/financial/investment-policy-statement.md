## 1. Investment Objectives
The primary objective of the Trust is **Long-Term Growth and Preservation**.
*   **Time Horizon**: Perpetual (365+ years).
*   **Return Goal**: To exceed the rate of inflation (CPI) by at least 4% annually, net of fees and taxes.

## 2. Risk Tolerance
The Trust has a **High** capacity for risk due to its perpetual time horizon, but a **Moderate** tolerance for volatility.
*   **Acceptable Volatility**: The Trust accepts short-term market fluctuations in exchange for long-term appreciation.
*   **Permanent Loss**: The Trust seeks to avoid permanent impairment of capital (bankruptcy/total loss).

## 3. Asset Allocation Targets

| Asset Class | Target Allocation | Range | Benchmark |
| :--- | :---: | :---: | :--- |
| **Operating Businesses** | 40% | 20-60% | GDP Growth |
| **Real Estate** | 40% | 20-60% | REIT Index |
| **Public Equities** | 15% | 5-25% | S&P 500 |
| **Cash/Equivalents** | 5% | 2-10% | 3-Mo T-Bill |

## 4. Investment Guidelines
*   **Diversification**: No single investment (outside of the Family Business) shall exceed 10% of the total portfolio.
*   **Liquidity**: The Trust shall maintain sufficient liquidity to meet anticipated distributions (HEMS) for 12 months.
*   **Prohibited Assets**: The Trust shall not invest in:
    *   Unregistered securities (without legal review).
    *   Businesses engaged in unethical activities.

## 5. Review and Monitoring
The Investment Trustee shall review the portfolio **Quarterly** and rebalance if allocations drift outside the allowable range.
