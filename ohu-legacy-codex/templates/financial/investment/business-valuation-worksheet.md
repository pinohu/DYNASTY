# Business Valuation Worksheet

## Purpose

This template helps estimate the value of a business using multiple valuation methods. It's useful for buying/selling businesses, estate planning, or strategic planning.

## Instructions

1. **Gather Financials**: Collect 3-5 years of financial statements
2. **Calculate Metrics**: Compute key financial ratios and multiples
3. **Apply Methods**: Use multiple valuation methods
4. **Reconcile**: Average or weight different methods
5. **Document Assumptions**: Record all assumptions used
