# Annual Operating Budget Template

## Purpose

This template establishes the financial plan for the upcoming year. It sets targets for revenue and limits for expenses, serving as a benchmark for performance monitoring.

## Instructions

1.  **Forecast Revenue**: Estimate sales based on historical data and growth targets.
2.  **Estimate Expenses**: Project fixed and variable costs.
3.  **Set Targets**: Define monthly targets to track progress.
4.  **Review**: Approve the budget before the fiscal year begins.
