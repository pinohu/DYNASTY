# Capital Budget Template

## Purpose

This template is used to plan for significant long-term investments (Capital Expenditures or CapEx), such as equipment, property, or major software upgrades. It separates these large, one-time costs from the operating budget.

## Instructions

1.  **Identify Needs**: List required assets or improvements.
2.  **Estimate Costs**: Obtain quotes or estimates.
3.  **Justification**: Explain the ROI or necessity of the purchase.
4.  **Timing**: Schedule when the cash outlay will occur.

## Template

```markdown
# CAPITAL EXPENDITURE (CAPEX) BUDGET

**Entity**: [ENTITY NAME]
**Year**: [YEAR]

| Item Description | Justification/ROI | Estimated Cost | Purchase Date | Priority (H/M/L) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [ITEM 1] | [REASON] | $[AMOUNT] | [DATE] | [PRIORITY] |
| [ITEM 2] | [REASON] | $[AMOUNT] | [DATE] | [PRIORITY] |
| [ITEM 3] | [REASON] | $[AMOUNT] | [DATE] | [PRIORITY] |

## TOTAL CAPEX BUDGET: $[AMOUNT]

## FUNDING SOURCE

- [ ] Operating Cash Flow: $[AMOUNT]
- [ ] Financing/Loans: $[AMOUNT]
- [ ] Capital Contribution: $[AMOUNT]

---

**Approved By**: [NAME]
**Date**: [DATE]
```
