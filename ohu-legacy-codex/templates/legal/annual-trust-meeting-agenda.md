# Annual Trust Meeting Agenda

## Purpose
To document the annual review of the Trust's performance and compliance.

---

## 1. Call to Order
- [ ] Confirm quorum of Trustees.
- [ ] Approve minutes from last meeting.

## 2. Financial Review
- [ ] Review Balance Sheet and Income Statement.
- [ ] Review Investment Performance vs. Benchmarks.
- [ ] Review Investment Policy Statement (IPS) compliance.
- [ ] **Action**: Rebalance portfolio if needed.

## 3. Distribution Review
- [ ] Ratify all distributions made during the year.
- [ ] Review beneficiary requests/needs for upcoming year.
- [ ] Verify "Productive Citizen" status of beneficiaries.

## 4. Administrative Matters
- [ ] Confirm all tax filings (1041, K-1) are complete.
- [ ] Confirm all Crummey Notices were sent and acknowledged.
- [ ] Review Trustee fees and expenses.

## 5. Strategic Planning
- [ ] Discuss major upcoming liquidity events (sales, acquisitions).
- [ ] Discuss changes in family circumstances (births, marriages).

## 6. Adjournment
- [ ] Set date for next meeting.
