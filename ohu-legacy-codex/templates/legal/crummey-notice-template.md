## CRUMMEY WITHDRAWAL NOTICE

**Date**: _______________

**To**: [Beneficiary Name]
[Beneficiary Address]
[City, State ZIP]

**Re**: Crummey Withdrawal Right - [Trust Name]

Dear [Beneficiary Name],

This notice informs you of your right to withdraw funds from the **[Trust Name]** (the "Trust").

### Gift Information

On **[Gift Date]**, a gift of **$[Amount]** was made to the Trust for your benefit. Under the terms of the Trust, you have the right to withdraw up to **$[Amount]** from the Trust.

### Your Withdrawal Right

- **Withdrawal Amount**: Up to $[Amount]
- **Withdrawal Period**: 30 days from the date of this notice
- **Expiration Date**: [Date + 30 days]
- **Lapse**: If you do not exercise your right within 30 days, it will lapse and the funds will remain in the Trust for your future benefit

### How to Exercise Your Right

If you wish to exercise your withdrawal right, you must notify the Trustee **in writing** within 30 days of the date of this notice.

**Send your written request to**:

[Trustee Name]
[Trustee Address]
[City, State ZIP]
[Phone Number]
[Email Address]

**Your written request must include**:
- Your full name
- Your address
- The amount you wish to withdraw (up to $[Amount])
- Your signature
- Date of request

### Important Notes

1. **Legal Requirement**: This notice is required by law to qualify the gift for the annual gift tax exclusion. Without this notice, the gift may be subject to gift tax.

2. **No Obligation**: You are **not required** to withdraw the funds. This is your choice.

3. **Future Benefit**: If you do not withdraw the funds, they will remain in the Trust for your future benefit, subject to the Trust's distribution provisions.

4. **This Gift Only**: This withdrawal right applies only to this specific gift made on [Gift Date]. It does not affect your rights regarding other trust assets or future gifts.

5. **Questions**: If you have any questions about this notice or your withdrawal right, please contact the Trustee at the address above.

### Acknowledgment

Please sign and return the acknowledgment below to confirm you received this notice. You are not required to exercise your withdrawal right, but we need confirmation that you received this notice.
