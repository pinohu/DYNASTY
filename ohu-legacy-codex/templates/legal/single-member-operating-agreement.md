*   **Manager**: [Manager Name] (e.g., PNR Holdings LLC or Individual)

*   **Initial Contribution**: $[Amount]

## 5. Distributions
Distributions of Net Cash Flow shall be made to the Member at such times and in such amounts as the Manager determines.

## 6. Liability of Member
The Member shall not be liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company, except as provided by law.

## 7. Charging Order Protection
The parties agree that the exclusive remedy of a judgment creditor of the Member shall be a Charging Order against the Member's transferable interest. The creditor shall have no right to seize assets, vote, or participate in management.

## 8. Dissolution
The Company shall dissolve upon the written consent of the Member or the entry of a decree of judicial dissolution.
