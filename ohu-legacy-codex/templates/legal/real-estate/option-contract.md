# Real Estate Option Contract Template

## Purpose

This template creates an option to purchase real estate, giving the option holder the right (but not obligation) to buy the property at a specified price within a set time period. This is useful for controlling property without immediate purchase.

**IMPORTANT**: Option contracts must comply with state real estate laws. Review with qualified counsel.

## Instructions

1. **Set Option Price**: Amount paid for the option (option fee)
2. **Set Exercise Price**: Price to purchase if option is exercised
3. **Set Term**: Time period to exercise option
4. **Define Terms**: Purchase terms if option is exercised
5. **Review with Attorney**: Must be reviewed by qualified counsel

## Template

Optionor hereby grants to Optionee the exclusive right and option to purchase the Property on the terms set forth herein.

### 2. Option Price

Optionee has paid Optionor an option fee of **$[AMOUNT]** (the "Option Price") for the right to purchase the Property. This Option Price is:
- [ ] Non-refundable
- [ ] Credited toward purchase price if option is exercised
- [ ] [OTHER TERMS]

If Optionee exercises this option, the purchase price shall be **$[AMOUNT]** (the "Exercise Price").

### 4. Option Term

This option may be exercised at any time on or before [DATE] (the "Expiration Date"). If not exercised by the Expiration Date, this option shall expire and be of no further effect.

### 5. Method of Exercise

Optionee may exercise this option by:
- Delivering written notice to Optionor at the address above
- Paying the Exercise Price (or portion as specified)
- [OTHER EXERCISE REQUIREMENTS]

**Purchase Price**: $[AMOUNT] (Exercise Price)

**Earnest Money**: $[AMOUNT] (in addition to Option Price)

**Closing Date**: Within [NUMBER] days of exercise notice

**Financing**: [CONTINGENT/NOT CONTINGENT] upon financing

**Contingencies**: [LIST CONTINGENCIES - inspection, appraisal, etc.]

### 7. Title

Optionor shall provide marketable title free of liens and encumbrances except:
- [LIST PERMITTED EXCEPTIONS]

---

## OPTIONOR OBLIGATIONS

### 8. Property Maintenance

During the option term, Optionor shall:
- Maintain Property in current condition
- Not encumber Property with additional liens
- Allow Optionee reasonable access for inspections
- [OTHER OBLIGATIONS]

### 9. No Sale to Third Parties

Optionor shall not sell, lease, or encumber the Property to any third party during the option term without Optionee's written consent.

---

## OPTIONEE OBLIGATIONS

### 10. Option Price Payment

Optionee has paid the Option Price as set forth in Section 2.

### 11. Exercise Notice

If Optionee wishes to exercise, Optionee must provide written notice and payment as specified in Section 5.

### 12. Optionee Default

If Optionee fails to exercise by Expiration Date:
- Option expires
- Option Price is forfeited (if non-refundable)
- Optionor has no further obligations

### 13. Optionor Default

If Optionor breaches this Agreement:
- Optionee may seek specific performance
- Optionee may recover damages
- Option Price shall be refunded

## Notes

- **Option vs. Purchase**: An option gives the right to buy but not the obligation. The buyer can walk away and lose only the option fee.

- **Option Price**: Typically 1-5% of purchase price, but negotiable.

- **Exercise Price**: Can be fixed or based on appraisal at time of exercise.

- **Assignment**: Consider whether option should be assignable to third parties.

- **Tax Implications**: Option fees may have tax consequences. Consult tax advisor.
