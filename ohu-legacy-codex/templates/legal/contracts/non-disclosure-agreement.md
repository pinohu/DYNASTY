# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) Template

## Purpose

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement protects confidential information shared between two parties. It is essential when discussing potential partnerships, hiring contractors, or sharing business secrets.

## Instructions

1.  **Definition**: Ensure the definition of "Confidential Information" covers your specific data (e.g., customer lists, code, financial data).
2.  **Term**: Set a duration for the confidentiality obligation (typically 2-5 years, or indefinite for trade secrets).
3.  **Exclusions**: Standard exclusions (publicly known info, etc.) are included.

## Template

```markdown
# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**Effective Date**: [DATE]

**BETWEEN**:
1.  **[PARTY A NAME]** ("Party A")
2.  **[PARTY B NAME]** ("Party B")

## 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one party ("Disclosing Party") to the other ("Receiving Party"), whether orally or in writing, that is designated as confidential or should reasonably be understood to be confidential. This includes, but is not limited to:
-   Business plans and strategies
-   Customer and vendor lists
-   Financial information
-   Intellectual property, code, and technical data
-   Trade secrets

## 2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:
(a) Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence;
(b) Not disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent;
(c) Use such information solely for the purpose of the business relationship between the parties;
(d) Protect such information with the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information (but no less than reasonable care).

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does not include information that:
(a) Is or becomes publicly known through no breach by the Receiving Party;
(b) Was in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure;
(c) Is rightfully received from a third party without a duty of confidentiality;
(d) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party.

## 4. TERM

This Agreement applies to information disclosed within [NUMBER] years of the Effective Date. The duty of confidentiality shall survive for a period of [NUMBER] years from the date of disclosure. Trade secrets shall remain confidential indefinitely.

## 5. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon termination of the relationship or request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all copies of Confidential Information.

## 6. NO LICENSE

Nothing in this Agreement grants any license or right to the Confidential Information or intellectual property of the Disclosing Party.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

```
