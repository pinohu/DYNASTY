# Member Resolution Template

## Purpose
This template provides a standard format for member resolutions documenting important decisions and actions.

## Template

**RESOLUTION OF MEMBERS OF [LLC NAME]**

Date: [Date]

**RESOLVED**, that the Members of [LLC Name], LLC (the "Company") hereby resolve as follows:

**[Description of resolution - Be specific and detailed]**

**Background:**
[Provide background and context for the resolution]

**Rationale:**
[Explain the reasoning behind the resolution]

**Authorization:**
The Members hereby authorize [Name/Title] to take all actions necessary to implement this resolution.

**Effective Date:**
This resolution shall be effective as of [Date].

This resolution is adopted by the Members holding a majority of the membership interests of the Company.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the Members have executed this resolution as of the date first written above.

[Member Name]                    [Member Name]
_________________________         _________________________
Signature                         Signature

Date: _______________            Date: _______________

---

## Usage Instructions

1. **Complete Template**: Fill in all bracketed information
2. **Be Specific**: Clearly describe the resolution
3. **Provide Context**: Include background and rationale
4. **Obtain Signatures**: All required members must sign
5. **File Securely**: Store executed resolution securely

## Common Uses

- Approve major business decisions
- Authorize significant transactions
- Adopt policies
- Approve distributions
- Authorize contracts
- Other formal actions

## Notes

- Resolutions document important decisions
- Must be signed by required members
- Should be filed with entity records
- Retain permanently
- Review with legal counsel for major decisions
