# Trust Amendment Template

## Purpose

This template is used to formally amend an existing Trust Agreement. It allows the Grantor or Trust Protector (depending on the trust type) to modify specific provisions without restating the entire trust.

**IMPORTANT**: Verify the trust document allows for amendments and who holds the power to amend.

## Instructions

1.  **Identify Trust**: Clearly reference the original trust name and date.
2.  **Authority**: Cite the specific article/section granting amendment power.
3.  **Changes**: Clearly state the text to be deleted, added, or replaced.
4.  **Ratification**: Confirm that all other terms remain in effect.

## Template

```markdown
# AMENDMENT TO THE [TRUST NAME]

**Date**: [DATE]

**THIS AMENDMENT** is made to the [TRUST NAME], originally dated [ORIGINAL DATE] (the "Trust").

## RECITALS

A. The Trust was established by [GRANTOR NAME] as Grantor.
B. Article [NUMBER] of the Trust reserves the right to amend the Trust to [GRANTOR/TRUST PROTECTOR].
C. The undersigned desires to amend the Trust as set forth below.

## AMENDMENT

**FIRST**: Article [NUMBER], Section [NUMBER] is hereby amended to read in its entirety as follows:

> "[INSERT NEW TEXT OF PROVISION]"

**SECOND**: Article [NUMBER] is hereby deleted in its entirety.

**THIRD**: A new Article [NUMBER] is added as follows:

> "[INSERT NEW TEXT]"

## RATIFICATION

Except as expressly amended hereby, the Trust is ratified and confirmed and shall remain in full force and effect.

---

**Signatures**:

Grantor/Trust Protector: __________________________ Date: __________

**Witnesses** (if required):

Witness 1: __________________________

Witness 2: __________________________

**Notary Acknowledgement**:
[State specific notary block]
```
