# Crummey Notice Template

## Purpose
This template provides a standard format for Crummey withdrawal notices required for ILIT and Children's Crummey Trust annual exclusion gifting.

## Template

**CRUMMEY WITHDRAWAL NOTICE**

Date: [Date]

To: [Beneficiary Name]
Address: [Beneficiary Address]

**RE: Withdrawal Right Notice - [Trust Name]**

Dear [Beneficiary Name],

This notice is provided pursuant to the terms of the [Trust Name] (the "Trust") and applicable tax law.

**Gift Information:**
- **Gift Amount**: $[Amount]
- **Gift Date**: [Date]
- **Gift Source**: [Grantor Name]

**Withdrawal Right:**
You have the right to withdraw the following amount from the Trust:

**Withdrawal Amount**: $[Amount]

**Withdrawal Period:**
You may exercise this withdrawal right at any time during the 30-day period beginning on [Start Date] and ending on [End Date] (the "Withdrawal Period").

**How to Exercise:**
To exercise your withdrawal right, you must deliver written notice to the Trustee at the address below on or before [End Date].

**Trustee Contact Information:**
[Trustee Name]
[Trustee Address]
[Trustee Phone]
[Trustee Email]

**Important Notes:**
- If you do not exercise your withdrawal right during the Withdrawal Period, your right will expire and the gift will remain in the Trust.
- This withdrawal right is provided to ensure the gift qualifies for the annual gift tax exclusion.
- Please consult with your tax advisor regarding the tax implications of exercising or not exercising this withdrawal right.

**Questions:**
If you have any questions regarding this notice or your withdrawal right, please contact the Trustee at the address above.

Sincerely,

[Trustee Name]
Trustee, [Trust Name]

---

## Usage Instructions

1. **Complete Template**: Fill in all bracketed information
2. **Send Promptly**: Send notice immediately when gift is made
3. **Track Responses**: Document all beneficiary responses
4. **Retain Records**: Keep copies of all notices and responses
5. **File Securely**: Store in trust administration files

## Compliance Notes

- Must be sent within reasonable time of gift
- Must provide at least 30-day withdrawal period
- Must be in writing
- Must be delivered to beneficiary
- Must be documented and retained
