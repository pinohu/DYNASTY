# Appendix A: Quick Reference Guides

## Introduction

This appendix provides high-level checklists for the recurring rhythms of the The Legacy Codex. These guides are designed to be printed or integrated into your project management software (ClickUp/SuiteDash) to ensure consistency and completeness in daily operations.

---

## A.1 Daily Checklist

### Executive / Leadership
- [ ] **Review Dashboard:** Check key metrics (Cash, Sales, Operations) in SuiteDash.
- [ ] **Triage Communications:** Clear urgent emails/Slack messages (Inbox Zero goal).
- [ ] **Stand-up:** 15-minute sync with key direct reports (if applicable).
- [ ] **Deep Work:** 2-4 hours of uninterrupted strategic work on Horizon 1 or 2 projects.
- [ ] **Review Priorities:** Set top 3 priorities for tomorrow before leaving.

### Operations
- [ ] **System Check:** Verify all automated workflows (Make/Zapier) are running without errors.
- [ ] **Customer Service:** Ensure all support tickets are responded to within SLA.
- [ ] **Fulfillment:** Review daily service delivery or product shipment status.

---

## A.2 Weekly Task List

### Financial
- [ ] **Cash Flow Review:** Review bank balances and upcoming payables.
- [ ] **Invoicing:** Send out all due invoices; follow up on past due accounts.
- [ ] **Expense Review:** Approve pending expenses and receipts.

### Marketing & Sales
- [ ] **Content Review:** Review and approve social media/content calendar for next week.
- [ ] **Ad Performance:** Review paid ad metrics (ROAS, CPA) and adjust bids/creative.
- [ ] **Pipeline Review:** Review sales pipeline status and deal velocity.

### Team
- [ ] **Team Meeting:** Weekly all-hands or department sync (60-90 mins).
- [ ] **1:1s:** Conduct one-on-one meetings with direct reports.
- [ ] **Training:** 1 hour of professional development or training.

---

## A.3 Monthly Requirements

### Financial
- [ ] **Bookkeeping:** Reconcile all bank and credit card accounts.
- [ ] **Financial Statements:** Generate and review P&L and Balance Sheet.
- [ ] **Budget Variance:** Compare actuals vs. budget; investigate variances >10%.

### Strategic
- [ ] **KPI Review:** Deep dive into monthly KPIs; adjust tactics if off-track.
- [ ] **Vendor Review:** Review performance of key vendors/contractors.
- [ ] **Compliance:** Check for any due tax filings or license renewals.

### Family Governance
- [ ] **Family Meeting:** Monthly family council check-in (financials & updates).
- [ ] **Education:** Review children's business education progress.

---

## A.4 Quarterly Priorities

### Strategic Planning
- [ ] **Quarterly Review:** Review previous quarter's goals (OKRs/Rocks).
- [ ] **Quarterly Planning:** Set goals and priorities for the upcoming quarter.
- [ ] **SWOT Analysis:** Quick update of Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats.

### Financial & Tax
- [ ] **Estimated Taxes:** Calculate and pay quarterly estimated taxes.
- [ ] **Distribution:** Determine and execute quarterly profit distributions (if applicable).
- [ ] **Asset Allocation:** Review investment portfolio and rebalance if needed.

### Operations
- [ ] **Tech Audit:** Review software subscriptions and usage.
- [ ] **SOP Review:** Update one core SOP area (rotate areas each quarter).

---

## A.5 Annual Calendar

### Q1 (Jan-Mar)
- [ ] **Annual Kickoff:** Team alignment on annual goals.
- [ ] **Tax Prep:** Finalize books for previous year; send data to CPA.
- [ ] **Performance Reviews:** Conduct annual team performance reviews.

### Q2 (Apr-Jun)
- [ ] **Strategic Retreat:** Family council annual retreat (Vision & Legacy).
- [ ] **Insurance Review:** Audit all insurance policies for coverage adequacy.
- [ ] **Legal Review:** Review entity structures and minute books.

### Q3 (Jul-Sep)
- [ ] **Growth Planning:** Begin preliminary planning for next year's growth initiatives.
- [ ] **Asset Protection:** Review asset protection structures and estate plan.
- [ ] **Education:** Plan upcoming year's educational curriculum for children.

### Q4 (Oct-Dec)
- [ ] **Budgeting:** Create detailed budget for the upcoming year.
- [ ] **Tax Planning:** End-of-year tax planning meeting with CPA (before Dec 31).
- [ ] **Philanthropy:** Execute end-of-year charitable giving.
- [ ] **Holiday:** Team and client appreciation/gifting.
