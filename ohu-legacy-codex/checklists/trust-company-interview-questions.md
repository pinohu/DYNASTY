# Trust Company Interview Questions

## Purpose
Use this questionnaire when interviewing potential South Dakota Corporate Trustees.

**Trust Company**: __________________________
**Contact Person**: __________________________
**Date**: __________________________

---

## 1. Capabilities & Experience
- [ ] How long have you been chartered in South Dakota?
- [ ] What is your total Assets Under Administration (AUA)?
- [ ] Do you specialize in Directed Trusts?
- [ ] How many Dynasty Trusts do you currently administer?

## 2. Fees & Costs
- [ ] What is your annual administrative fee? (Flat fee or % of assets?)
- [ ] Are there setup/onboarding fees?
- [ ] Do you charge extra for holding non-traditional assets (LLCs, Real Estate)?
- [ ] What are the termination/exit fees if we move the trust?

## 3. Operations & Service
- [ ] Will we have a dedicated trust officer?
- [ ] What is your turnaround time for distribution requests? (24h, 48h, 1 week?)
- [ ] Do you have an online portal for viewing trust assets?
- [ ] How do you handle tax reporting (Form 1041)? Do you prepare it or do we?

## 4. Flexibility
- [ ] Are you comfortable holding a single LLC (PNR Holdings) as the primary asset?
- [ ] Do you require an annual review of the underlying LLC assets?
- [ ] How do you handle "Decanting" requests?
