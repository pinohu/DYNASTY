## 3. Entity-Specific Requirements

### **Dynasty Trust (South Dakota)**
*   **Annual Fee:** Pay South Dakota Trustee fee annually.
*   **Trust Protector:** Must review trustee performance annually.
*   **Distribution Committee:** Document all distribution decisions (even if "No Distribution").

### **PNR Holdings LLC (Wyoming)**
*   **Registered Agent:** Maintain active WY Registered Agent.
*   **Operating Agreement:** Update Schedule A if asset ownership changes.

### **CXI LLC (S-Corp)**
*   **Payroll:** Must pay "Reasonable Salary" to officers before taking distributions.
*   **Accountable Plan:** Reimburse expenses quarterly to avoid taxable income.

### **Kwode LLC (Tech/Hybrid)**
*   **Asset Protection:** Verify E&O insurance limits cover asset value annually.
*   **Cash Sweep:** Execute monthly "Cash Sweep" to PNR to minimize liability exposure.
*   **DBA Renewal:** Ensure "Notroom" and "TaxEar" trade names are active.

### **Neat Circle LLC (Automation)**
*   **Cyber Liability:** Maintain active Tech E&O and Cyber insurance.
*   **SaaS Tax:** Monitor nexus for sales tax on digital services.

### **Obuke LLC (Series)**
*   **Bank Accounts:** Ensure each Series (1-10) has a separate bank account (or strictly segregated sub-account). **Co-mingling breaks the Series protection.**
*   **Deeds:** Ensure properties are titled "Obuke LLC - Series X".

### **Lodging Connections LLC (Hospitality)**
*   **Occupancy Tax:** File monthly/quarterly hotel taxes with local municipality.
*   **Licenses:** Renew Short-Term Rental permits annually.

### **Origin Eyes (501c3)**
*   **Public Support:** Monitor "Public Support Test" (33% from public) to avoid Private Foundation status (unless intended).
*   **Donation Receipts:** Send receipt letters for any donation >$250.

## 4. The "Hidden" Commercial Deadlines
*These are not government mandates, but if you miss them, your infrastructure breaks.*

*   **Domain Name Renewals:** (Critical for `torimedia.com` / `neatcircle.com`)
    *   *Action:* Set all to "Auto-Renew" but **Audit Credit Card on File** annually (e.g., Jan 5).
    *   *Risk:* If card fails, you lose the domain and email overnight.
*   **Registered Agent Service Fees:**
    *   *Action:* Pay the 3rd party service (e.g., Wyoming Agents) that receives your legal mail. This is separate from the State fee.
    *   *Date:* Usually anniversary of formation.
*   **Lender Compliance (Obuke LLC):**
    *   *Action:* Submit Annual Tax Returns and P&L to mortgage lenders (DSCR loans often require this).
    *   *Date:* Usually within 30 days of tax filing (e.g., May 15).
*   **Trademark Maintenance (ToriMedia):**
    *   *Action:* File Section 8 Declaration (Between Year 5-6) and Renewal (Year 10).
    *   *Date:* Specific to registration date.
*   **Data Backup Audit:**
    *   *Action:* **Test a Restore.** actually try to recover a file from backup to prove it works.
    *   *Date:* Quarterly (e.g., Jan 1, Apr 1, Jul 1, Oct 1).

## 5. Trigger-Based Compliance (The "Gotchas")
*Immediate action required if these occur:*

1.  **BOI Report Update (FinCEN):**
    *   *Trigger:* Principal moves house (change of address) OR gets a new Driver's License.
    *   *Deadline:* **30 Days** from the change.
    *   *Risk:* $500/day fine.
2.  **New Asset Acquisition:**
    *   *Trigger:* Buying a new property or vehicle.
    *   *Action:* Update "Schedule A" of the Trust or PNR Holdings Operating Agreement immediately.
    *   *Risk:* Asset is not protected if not listed.
3.  **New Hire:**
    *   *Trigger:* Hiring an employee.
    *   *Action:* File I-9 and W-4 immediately. Add to Workers Comp.
4.  **Lawsuit/Threat:**
    *   *Trigger:* Receiving a legal letter.
    *   *Action:* Notify Insurance Carrier and Attorney immediately. DO NOT REPLY.
