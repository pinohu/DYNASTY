# Trust Funding Checklist

## Purpose
To ensure the South Dakota Dynasty Trust is properly funded and recognized as a legal entity.

**Trust Name**: __________________________
**EIN**: __________________________

---

## Phase 1: Initial Setup
- [ ] **Trust Deed Signed**: Notarized signatures of Grantors and Trustees.
- [ ] **EIN Obtained**: Form SS-4 filed with IRS (Type: Irrevocable Trust).
- [ ] **Bank Account Opened**: Checking account in Trust's name.

## Phase 2: Initial Funding ("The Seed")
- [ ] **Gift Letter**: Letter from Grantors stating the intent to gift cash.
- [ ] **Check/Wire**: $10,000 transferred from Grantor personal account to Trust account.
- [ ] **Deposit Receipt**: Saved in permanent trust records.

## Phase 3: Asset Transfer (LLC Interests)
- [ ] **Assignment of Interest**: Document transferring 99.9% of PNR Holdings LLC to the Trust.
- [ ] **Amended Operating Agreement**: PNR Holdings LLC agreement updated to show Trust as member.
- [ ] **Membership Certificate**: Issued to the Trust.

## Phase 4: Insurance Policy Transfer (If applicable)
- [ ] **Change of Ownership Form**: Filed with insurance carrier.
- [ ] **Change of Beneficiary Form**: Trust named as beneficiary.
- [ ] **Confirmation**: Received from carrier.

---

**Verification**:
- [ ] All documents scanned to digital vault.
- [ ] Originals stored in fireproof safe.
- [ ] CPA notified of transfers for tax reporting (Form 709).
