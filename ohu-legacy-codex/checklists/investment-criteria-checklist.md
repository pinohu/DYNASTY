# Investment Criteria Checklist

## Purpose
Use this checklist to screen ANY potential investment or business acquisition. If it doesn't pass, we don't invest.

**Investment Name**: __________________________
**Cost**: $__________

---

## Phase 1: The Gatekeepers (Must be YES)
- [ ] **Cash Flow**: Does this generate positive cash flow immediately (or within 6 months)?
- [ ] **Control**: Do we have controlling interest (or a clear path to it)?
- [ ] **Simplicity**: Can we explain the business model on one page?
- [ ] **Ethics**: Is this business moral and ethical?

## Phase 2: The Moat (Competitive Advantage)
- [ ] **Entry Barrier**: Is it hard for competitors to copy this?
- [ ] **Pricing Power**: Can we raise prices without losing customers?
- [ ] **Stickiness**: Is it hard for customers to leave?

## Phase 3: The Numbers
- [ ] **ROI**: Is the projected ROI > 15%?
- [ ] **Margin**: Is the net profit margin > 20% (for services/software)?
- [ ] **Payback**: Is the payback period < 3 years?

## Phase 4: The Exit
- [ ] **Liquidity**: Can we sell this asset easily if needed?
- [ ] **Buyer Pool**: Is there a clear pool of potential buyers?
- [ ] **Legacy**: If we never sell, is it worth holding forever?

---

**Decision**:
[ ] **PASS** (Proceed to Due Diligence)
[ ] **FAIL** (Reject Investment)
