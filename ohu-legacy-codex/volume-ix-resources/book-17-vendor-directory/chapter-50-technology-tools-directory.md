# Chapter 50: Technology & Tools Directory

## Introduction

The Ohu Family Enterprise is built on a "Digital Backbone." We leverage technology to replace labor, enforce process, and scale without chaos. This chapter is the definitive inventory of the Empire's Tech Stack.

It is not just a list of tools; it is a guide to *why* we use them and *how* they fit together. We prioritize **Integration** (everything talks to SuiteDash) and **Automation** (everything is triggered by data).

## 50.1 Software Platforms

### The Core (The "Holy Trinity")

**1. SuiteDash (The BOS)**
-   **Purpose:** CRM, Project Management, Billing, Client Portal.
-   **Status:** Mandatory for all OpCos.
-   **Key Features:** White-labeling, Automations, Secure File Exchange.

**2. QuickBooks Online (The Ledger)**
-   **Purpose:** Accounting and Financial Reporting.
-   **Status:** Mandatory.
-   **Integration:** Syncs with SuiteDash for invoicing.

**3. Boost.space (The Data Hub)**
-   **Purpose:** Master Data Management (MDM). Syncs data between all other tools.
-   **Status:** The "Brain" of the automation network.

### Operational Tools

**GoZen Forms / Formaloo**
-   **Purpose:** Advanced data collection (Surveys, Quizzes, Intake).
-   **Use Case:** Client onboarding questionnaires.

**Acumbamail**
-   **Purpose:** Email Marketing and SMS.
-   **Use Case:** Newsletter blasts and drip campaigns.

## 50.2 Online Services

### Hosting & Web

**20i / Bunny.net**
-   **Purpose:** High-performance hosting and CDN.
-   **Use Case:** Hosting OpCo websites and directory sites.

**Publit.io**
-   **Purpose:** Digital Asset Management (DAM).
-   **Use Case:** Hosting images and videos for marketing.

### Communication

**Slack / Discord**
-   **Purpose:** Internal team communication.
-   **Rule:** "No internal emails."

**Google Workspace**
-   **Purpose:** Email (Gmail), Docs, Sheets.
-   **Standard:** Professional business email (@company.com).

## 50.3 AI & Automation

### The Automation Engine

**Make.com (formerly Integromat)**
-   **Purpose:** The "Glue" that connects APIs.
-   **Use Case:** "When a form is submitted (GoZen), create a client (SuiteDash) and send a contract (DocuSign)."

**KonnectzIT / Activepieces**
-   **Purpose:** Backup automation platforms (redundancy).

### AI Agents

**ChatGPT / Claude (LLMs)**
-   **Purpose:** Content generation, strategy, coding.
-   **Use Case:** Drafting blog posts, writing emails.

**Thoughtly**
-   **Purpose:** AI Voice Agents.
-   **Use Case:** Handling inbound calls for OpCos (24/7 reception).

**Katteb**
-   **Purpose:** Fact-checked AI article writing.
-   **Use Case:** SEO content for directories.
