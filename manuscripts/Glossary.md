# The Dynasty Glossary
## Definitions of Power, Law, and Strategy

| Term | Definition | Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **365-Year Vision** | The core philosophy of planning for 12-15 generations, utilizing the maximum perpetuity period of South Dakota trusts. | Foundation |
| **Asset Protection Firewall** | The legal strategy of separating high-risk assets (OpCos) from high-value assets (PropCos/HoldCos) to prevent liability contagion. | Legal |
| **BOS (Business Operating System)** | The central technological nervous system (SuiteDash) that manages all OpCos, clients, and data in one place. | Technology |
| **Charging Order Protection** | A legal protection where a creditor's only remedy against an LLC member is a lien on distributions, not seizure of assets or voting rights. | Legal |
| **Code Red** | The emergency protocol activated upon the death or incapacity of the Principal, triggering immediate succession steps. | Fortress Protocols |
| **Cost Segregation** | An engineering-based tax study that accelerates depreciation of real estate assets, creating massive tax deductions. | Financial |
| **Dynasty Trust** | An irrevocable trust sited in a jurisdiction like South Dakota that avoids the Rule Against Perpetuities and estate taxes forever. | Legal |
| **EA (Enrolled Agent)** | A federally licensed tax practitioner with unlimited rights to represent taxpayers before the IRS. | Tax |
| **HEMS Standard** | "Health, Education, Maintenance, and Support." The legal standard limiting trustee distributions to beneficiaries. | Trust |
| **HoldCo (Holding Company)** | A company (e.g., PNR Holdings LLC) that exists solely to own other companies or assets, providing a layer of anonymity and protection. | Legal |
| **OpCo (Operating Company)** | An active business entity (e.g., Notroom Services) that interacts with the public, takes risks, and generates cash flow. | Operations |
| **PropCo (Property Company)** | A passive entity (e.g., Obuke LLC) that owns real estate and leases it to the OpCo, protecting the asset from lawsuits. | Real Estate |
| **QSBS (Qualified Small Business Stock)** | A tax provision (Section 1202) allowing 100% tax-free gain on the sale of C-Corp stock held for 5+ years. | Tax |
| **REPS (Real Estate Professional Status)** | A tax status allowing real estate losses (depreciation) to offset active income (W-2 or business profit). | Tax |
| **RON (Remote Online Notarization)** | The digital notarization of documents via webcam, allowing for location-independent service delivery. | Operations |
| **Series LLC** | A unique LLC structure (available in WY, DE, etc.) where one "Master" LLC can spawn infinite "Cells" with separate liability shields. | Legal |
| **STR Loophole** | "Short-Term Rental Loophole." A tax strategy using average stays of 7 days or less to bypass passive loss limitations. | Tax |
| **SubTo (Subject-To)** | acquiring a property subject to the existing mortgage, taking title without assuming the debt. | Real Estate |
| **Triple-Net Lease (NNN)** | A commercial lease where the tenant (OpCo) pays all taxes, insurance, and maintenance, leaving pure profit for the landlord (PropCo). | Real Estate |
| **Wyoming Treasury** | The strategy of using a Wyoming LLC (PNR Holdings) as the "Bank" for the empire due to its superior privacy and charging order protections. | Legal |
