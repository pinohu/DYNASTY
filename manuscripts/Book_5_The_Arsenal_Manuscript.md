
# Chapter 48: Apex Tax Contracts

## 48.1 Family Employment Agreement (Audit-Proof)

**This Agreement** is made and entered into on [DATE], by and between:

**Employer:** [COMPANY NAME], a [STATE] [ENTITY TYPE] ("Company")
**Employee:** [CHILD NAME] ("Employee")

### 1. Employment Status
Company hereby employs Employee to perform the duties described below. This is a bona fide employment relationship. Employee is not an independent contractor.

### 2. Duties and Responsibilities
Employee shall perform the following legitimate business duties:
*   **Social Media Management:** Creating and posting content for business accounts.
*   **Office Maintenance:** Cleaning and organizing physical office space.
*   **Model:** Appearing in marketing materials (photos/videos).
*   **Data Entry:** Digitizing business cards and receipts.
*   **Board Service:** Serving as a Junior Advisor to the Family Council (if age appropriate).

### 3. Compensation
Company shall pay Employee at the rate of **$[RATE] per hour**.
*   This rate is based on market research for similar tasks in [LOCATION].
*   Total annual compensation shall not exceed the Standard Deduction for the current tax year ($14,600 for 2024) unless otherwise authorized.
*   Payment shall be made via [CHECK/DIRECT DEPOSIT] to Employee's custodial account.

### 4. Schedule and Tracking
*   Employee agrees to work approximately [HOURS] hours per week.
*   **CRITICAL:** Employee must log all hours worked in the Company Time Tracker (SuiteDash/Timesheet).
*   No pay shall be issued without a verified timesheet.

### 5. At-Will Employment
This employment is at-will and may be terminated by either party at any time.

**Landlord:** [HOMEOWNER NAME] ("Landlord")
**Tenant:** [COMPANY NAME] ("Tenant")

### 1. Property
Landlord agrees to rent to Tenant the residence located at:
[HOME ADDRESS]

### 2. Term (The "Augusta" Limit)
This lease is for a term of **[NUMBER] days** (must be 14 or fewer per year).
Dates of use: [LIST DATES, e.g., "Monthly Board Meeting on the 1st of each month"]

### 3. Purpose
Tenant shall use the Property for legitimate business purposes, including but not limited to:
*   Monthly Board of Directors Meetings
*   Strategic Planning Retreats
*   Shareholder Meetings
*   Audio/Video Recording Sessions

### 4. Rent
Tenant agrees to pay Landlord a total rent of **$[AMOUNT]**.
*   This rate is based on comparable commercial meeting space or high-end event rentals in the area.
*   (Example: 14 days x $1,000/day = $14,000 total).
