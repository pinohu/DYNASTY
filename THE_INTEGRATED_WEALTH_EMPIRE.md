Here's what happens when you combine these seven credentials under one roof:

### **1. Enrolled Agent (EA)**
- **What it is**: IRS-granted tax authority to represent taxpayers nationwide
- **Power**: Federal tax preparation, tax planning, IRS representation
- **Revenue**: $500–$5,000 per tax return; $1,000–$10,000 for tax planning

- **What it is**: License to give investment advice and manage portfolios

- **Power**: Remote closings, estate documents, business filings, loan signings
- **Revenue**: $25–$200 per notarization (scalable to hundreds per month)

- **What it is**: Professional who manages real estate transactions from contract to close
- **Power**: Streamline closings, manage compliance, coordinate all parties
- **Revenue**: $300–$600 per transaction (or monthly retainer)

## **CFP VS. SERIES 65: THE STRATEGIC CHOICE**

Most people assume you need a CFP® (Certified Financial Planner) to give investment advice.

You don't.

### **What CFP Gives You:**
- Brand recognition and credibility
- Comprehensive financial planning education
- Higher trust with high-net-worth clients
- 6–18 months of coursework + exam

### **What Series 65 Gives You:**
- **Legal authority to give investment advice** (same as CFP)
- Ability to charge AUM fees
- Ability to manage portfolios
- Ability to register an RIA
- 2–4 weeks of study + one exam

### **The Truth:**
**Series 65 provides the same legal authority as CFP for investment advisory—but faster and cheaper.**

CFP is a *designation*. Series 65 is a *license*.

For investment advice, you need the license. The designation is optional.

### **Your Best Path:**

✅ **Start with Series 65 + RIA**
- Pass Series 65 in 2–4 weeks
- Register your RIA in 30–60 days
- Begin offering investment services legally
- Charge AUM fees immediately

✅ **Add CFP Later (Optional)**
- After your practice is profitable
- For additional credibility
- To attract ultra-high-net-worth clients
- Not required for legal authority

**Bottom line**: Series 65 gets you operational in 90 days. CFP takes 6–18 months. Both allow investment advisory. Series 65 is the faster, smarter path.

### **Key Formulas to Memorize**

**CAPM (Capital Asset Pricing Model):**
```
Expected Return = Risk-Free Rate + Beta × (Market Return - Risk-Free Rate)
```

**Sharpe Ratio:**
```
Sharpe Ratio = (Portfolio Return - Risk-Free Rate) ÷ Standard Deviation
```

**Current Yield:**
```
Current Yield = Annual Interest ÷ Current Market Price
```

**Tax-Equivalent Yield:**
```
Tax-Equivalent Yield = Tax-Free Yield ÷ (1 - Tax Rate)
```

**Rule of 72:**
```
Years to Double = 72 ÷ Interest Rate
```
