## Strategic Architecture

### Multi-Entity Corporate Structure

**HoldCo (Parent & Capital Allocator)**
- Wyoming or Delaware LLC structure
- Owns equity in all subsidiaries
- Centralizes treasury and governance functions
- Capital allocation across business lines

**Four Operating Companies (OpCos)**
1. **Digital Services OpCo** - SuiteDash portal services, automation consulting
2. **Directories OpCo** - Brilliant Directories platforms, membership monetization
3. **SaaS & Tools OpCo** - Micro-SaaS development, API services
4. **Media & Education OpCo** - Content creation, VR courses, podcasts

**Supporting Entities**
- **PropCo** - Holds all real estate assets, charges rent to operating companies
- **IPCo** - Owns intellectual property, licenses to operating companies
- **Shared Services Center of Excellence** - Finance, legal, HR, data/AI, PMO
- **Optional 501(c)(3) Foundation** - Philanthropic initiatives

## Tax Optimization Framework

### OpCo/PropCo Structure Benefits
- Deductible rent payments from operating companies to property company
- Depreciation benefits concentrated in property entity
- Asset protection separating operational risks from real estate
- Enhanced financing through entity-specific optimization

### Real Estate Professional Status (REPS)
- Requirements: 750+ hours annually in real estate activities
- Benefits: Unlimited passive loss deductions, exemption from 3.8% Net Investment Income Tax
- Documentation: >50% of total working time in real estate trades

### 100% Bonus Depreciation Strategy
- Qualification: Property placed in service after January 19, 2025
- Cost segregation: 15-25% of building basis allocated to shorter-lived assets
- Tax impact: Substantial first-year deductions for immediate tax benefits

### S-Corp Elections
- Deadline: March 15, 2026 for 2025 elections (75 days for new entities)
- Benefits: Eliminate self-employment taxes on distributions
- Requirements: Reasonable salary documentation, transfer pricing compliance
