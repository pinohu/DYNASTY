# JoggAI - AI Avatar Video Generation Platform

## Overview
JoggAI is an advanced AI-powered video generation platform that transforms text, links, and images into professional videos featuring lifelike AI avatars. The platform specializes in creating engaging video content without requiring filming, editing, or voiceover work, making video production accessible to non-technical users. JoggAI stands out with its comprehensive avatar customization, multi-language support, and specialized tools for marketing video creation.

## Key Features
- **AI Avatar Creation**: Generate lifelike AI avatars from text prompts, photos, or clone your own likeness
- **Text-to-Video Conversion**: Transform scripts into professional videos with AI avatars
- **Multi-Language Support**: Create and translate videos in 90+ languages with advanced lip-syncing
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone your voice to create a digital twin for 24/7 representation
- **Product Video Ads**: Generate scroll-stopping video ads from URLs and product images
- **Static Image Animation**: Animate any photo into a realistic, speaking avatar
- **AI Script Writing**: AI-powered writing assistance for creating polished scripts
- **Video Translation**: Translate existing videos into multiple languages with lip-sync
- **Podcast Video Generation**: Convert links, blogs, PDFs into interview-style podcast videos
- **Template Library**: Industry-specific templates for various use cases
- **Batch Video Creation**: Generate multiple videos simultaneously for campaign scaling
- **AI-Assisted Editing**: Trim, style, subtitle, and animate videos with AI tools
- **Custom Avatar Library**: Access to 450+ ultra-realistic pre-made avatars
- **Mobile App**: Full-featured mobile application for video creation on-the-go
