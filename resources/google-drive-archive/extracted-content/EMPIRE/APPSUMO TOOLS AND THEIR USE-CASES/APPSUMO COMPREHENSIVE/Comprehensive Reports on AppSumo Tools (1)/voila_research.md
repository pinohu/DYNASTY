# Voilà - AI-Powered Browser Assistant

## Overview
Voilà is an AI-powered browser extension that serves as a personal assistant to help users create high-quality content, improve writing, get answers to questions, and boost productivity. The platform integrates seamlessly across multiple browsers and websites, providing instant access to AI capabilities without leaving your current workflow.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Content Creation
- **Instant Question Answering**: Get answers to any question directly from your browser
- **Content Generation**: Create blog posts, essays, marketing copy, and technical documents
- **Outline Creation**: Generate structured outlines for pitch decks, campaigns, and blog posts
- **Domain-Specific Content**: Specialized content for product development, UX, engineering, sales, and SEO

### Writing Enhancement
- **Grammar and Spelling**: Fix grammatical errors and spelling mistakes
- **Writing Improvement**: Enhance existing copy and content quality
- **Text Summarization**: Extract key information from any document
- **Translation Services**: Translate text between multiple languages
- **Tone Adjustment**: Generate content with appropriate tone (formal, casual, creative, urgent)

### Email Integration
- **Gmail Integration**: Native integration for crafting professional emails
- **Email Templates**: Quick generation of various email types
- **Response Assistance**: Help with thoughtful and timely email responses
- **Tone Matching**: Ensure emails capture the right professional vibe

### Browser Integration
- **Universal Compatibility**: Works across Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Brave browsers
- **Website Integration**: Functions on Shopify, LinkedIn, Facebook, Trello, Mailchimp, WordPress, Google Drive
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Convenient shortcuts for quick access and workflow acceleration
- **Non-Intrusive Design**: Seamlessly blends into existing workflows

### Privacy and Security
- **GDPR Compliant**: Meets European data protection standards
- **No Data Storage**: Content and AI conversations are not stored on Voilà's servers
- **Privacy Protection**: Confidential information remains private and secure
- **Local Processing**: Maintains user privacy while providing AI assistance
