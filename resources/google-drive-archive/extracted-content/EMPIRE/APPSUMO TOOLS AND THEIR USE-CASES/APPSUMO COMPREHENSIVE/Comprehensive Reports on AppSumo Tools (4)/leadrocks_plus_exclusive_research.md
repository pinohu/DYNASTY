# LeadRocks - Plus Exclusive Research

## Overview
LeadRocks - Plus Exclusive is an enhanced version of the LeadRocks B2B contact database and lead generation platform, exclusively available to AppSumo Plus members. The platform provides access to over 100 million verified business contacts, including emails, phone numbers, and LinkedIn profiles, along with advanced data enrichment and email outreach capabilities. This Plus Exclusive version offers significantly increased feature limits compared to the standard LeadRocks deal, making it a powerful tool for sales teams, marketers, and business development professionals who need high-volume lead generation capabilities.
