## Product Description
Cheat Layer is a revolutionary no-code business automation platform that leverages GPT-3 powered machine learning to create complex automations and turn any website into an API. The platform functions as a "personal software engineer" that can build white-label automations using natural language commands, action recorders, and OpenAI technology.

The tool addresses the challenge of complex business automation by making it accessible to non-technical users through AI-powered scripting and visual automation builders. Instead of requiring coding knowledge, users can describe what they want to automate in plain English, and Cheat Layer's AI creates the necessary automation scripts.
