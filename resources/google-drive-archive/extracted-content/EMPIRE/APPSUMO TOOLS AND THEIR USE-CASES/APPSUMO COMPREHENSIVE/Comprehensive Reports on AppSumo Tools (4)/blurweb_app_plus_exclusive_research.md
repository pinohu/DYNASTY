## Product Description
Blurweb App is a browser extension created to help blur sensitive information with just a single click during screen recording or live screen sharing. It allows users to draw rectangles to blur specific areas, hide tab titles & icons, blur selected text, and more. The extension is designed to solve the problem of accidentally sharing sensitive information during video calls, presentations, or screen recordings without requiring post-editing.
