## 3. Use Cases

### 1. Small Business Expense Management
**Scenario:** A consulting firm with 5 employees needs to track project expenses, client reimbursements, and operational costs.
**Implementation:** Team members scan receipts instantly using mobile app, AI categorizes by project/client, accountant receives real-time access to organized expenses, and monthly reports are generated automatically for billing and tax purposes.
**Benefit:** Reduces expense processing time from 8 hours monthly to 30 minutes while improving accuracy and client billing speed.

### 2. Freelancer Tax Preparation
**Scenario:** A freelance graphic designer needs to track business expenses throughout the year for tax deductions.
**Implementation:** Forward all e-receipts to SparkReceipt email, scan physical receipts with mobile app, AI separates business vs personal expenses, and export organized reports directly to tax software or accountant.
**Benefit:** Eliminates shoebox receipt storage, ensures no deductions are missed, and reduces tax preparation time by 75%.

### 3. Restaurant Chain Financial Management
**Scenario:** A restaurant owner with 3 locations needs to track supplier invoices, equipment purchases, and operational expenses across all locations.
**Implementation:** Create sub-accounts for each location, train staff to scan supplier receipts and invoices, use bulk upload for monthly statement processing, and generate location-specific P&L reports.
**Benefit:** Centralizes financial tracking, improves cost control visibility, and streamlines multi-location accounting processes.

### 4. E-commerce Business Inventory Tracking
**Scenario:** An online retailer needs to track product purchases, shipping costs, and business expenses for accurate profit margin calculations.
**Implementation:** Scan all supplier invoices and shipping receipts, categorize by product line or expense type, integrate with QuickBooks for automatic bookkeeping, and generate detailed cost analysis reports.
**Benefit:** Provides accurate COGS tracking, improves pricing decisions, and automates inventory expense management.

### 5. Professional Services Firm Client Billing
**Scenario:** A law firm needs to track client-related expenses for accurate billing and reimbursement.
**Implementation:** Team members tag receipts with client codes during scanning, AI categorizes by expense type, generate client-specific expense reports for billing, and maintain audit trail for compliance.
**Benefit:** Ensures all billable expenses are captured, improves client billing accuracy, and maintains professional documentation standards.

## 5. Limitations

### Technical Limitations
- **OCR Accuracy:** While 90%+ accurate, still requires occasional manual verification for complex receipts
- **Multi-Page Receipts:** Some users report challenges with restaurant receipts where tip/total are on separate lines
- **Currency Conversion:** Daily rate updates may not reflect exact transaction rates for international purchases
- **Bulk Processing:** Large batch uploads may experience slower processing times during peak usage
- **Mobile Dependency:** Optimal experience requires smartphone for receipt scanning

### Feature Limitations
- **Advanced Reporting:** Limited customization options compared to full accounting software
- **Inventory Tracking:** Not designed for detailed inventory management or cost basis tracking
- **Project Management:** Basic tagging system lacks advanced project cost allocation features
- **Multi-Currency Business:** Limited support for businesses operating in multiple base currencies
- **Audit Trail:** Basic document versioning without advanced compliance features

### Integration Limitations
- **Accounting Software:** Currently limited to QuickBooks integration, no Xero or other platforms
- **Bank Connections:** Requires manual bank statement upload, no direct bank API connections
- **CRM Integration:** No native integration with popular CRM systems for client expense tracking
- **Time Tracking:** No built-in time tracking for service-based businesses
- **Payroll Integration:** Cannot connect with payroll systems for employee expense reimbursement
