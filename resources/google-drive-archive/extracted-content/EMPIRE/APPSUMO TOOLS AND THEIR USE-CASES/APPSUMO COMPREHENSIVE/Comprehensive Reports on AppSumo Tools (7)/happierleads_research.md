## 2. Primary Features & Capabilities

### Core Visitor Identification Features:
- **Personal-Level Identification:** Identify exact individuals visiting websites (US-based visitors)
- **Company-Level Identification:** Identify companies and suggested contacts for outreach (international visitors)
- **Remote Worker Tracking:** Accurately track visitors working from home or using personal devices
- **Real-Time Visitor Monitoring:** Live tracking of website visitor activity and behavior patterns
- **Bot Filtering:** Automatic filtering of bots, ISPs, and non-qualifying traffic sources
- **Geographic Compliance:** GDPR-compliant reverse IP lookup for European visitors

### Lead Database & Prospecting:
- **Prospector Tool (Beta):** Access to 180M+ decision-makers and 60M+ companies database
- **Advanced Search Filters:** Find prospects by company size, industry, job title, revenue, location
- **Contact Data Enrichment:** Up to 20 attributes per contact including revenue, industry, founding year
- **Email Verification:** Scan and verify email validity for uploaded contact lists
- **Lead Qualification:** Automatic lead scoring and qualification based on visitor behavior

### Email Automation & Engagement:
- **Automated Email Campaigns:** Schedule and send drip campaigns to identified visitors
- **Lead Segmentation:** Segment leads based on behavior, demographics, and engagement criteria
- **Mailbox Rotation:** Scale cold email outreach by rotating between multiple mailboxes
- **AI Email Personalization:** AI-powered email content customization for higher engagement
- **Auto-Subscribe Feature:** Automatically add newly identified leads to outbound campaigns

### CRM Integration & Data Management:
- **Native CRM Integrations:** HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, Zoho, and other popular CRMs
- **API Access:** Advanced API for custom integrations and data enrichment
- **Zapier Integration:** Connect with 6,000+ apps for workflow automation
- **CSV Export/Import:** Bulk data management and contact list operations
- **Unlimited Data Storage:** No restrictions on data retention and storage

### Analytics & Reporting:
- **AI Session Summary:** Automated summaries of visitor sessions and behavior patterns
- **Conversion Tracking:** Monitor lead conversion rates and campaign performance
- **Engagement Analytics:** Track email open rates, click-through rates, and response rates
- **ROI Reporting:** Measure return on investment for lead generation campaigns
- **Custom Dashboards:** Personalized reporting dashboards for different user roles

### White-Label & Agency Features:

- **Reseller Program:** Opportunity to resell platform under agency branding

## 5. Limitations

### Technical Limitations:
- **Geographic Accuracy:** US visitors get personal-level identification; international visitors only company-level
- **Data Accuracy Concerns:** Some users report inconsistent data quality and false positives
- **Credit-Based System:** Monthly credit limits may restrict usage for high-traffic websites
- **Platform Maturity:** Some features still in beta stage with potential stability issues

### Feature Limitations:
- **Limited Social Media Integration:** Primarily focused on website visitors, limited social platform coverage
- **Email Deliverability:** Cold email success depends on sender reputation and email infrastructure
- **Lead Qualification Depth:** Basic qualification criteria compared to advanced marketing automation platforms
- **Real-Time Limitations:** Some data processing delays for immediate visitor identification

### Compliance & Privacy Concerns:
- **GDPR Restrictions:** Limited personal identification capabilities for European visitors
- **Privacy Regulations:** Ongoing changes in privacy laws may impact identification capabilities
- **Data Retention Policies:** Unclear long-term data storage and deletion policies
- **Consent Management:** Limited built-in consent management for privacy compliance

### Support & Documentation:
- **Customer Support Response:** Mixed reviews on support quality and response times
