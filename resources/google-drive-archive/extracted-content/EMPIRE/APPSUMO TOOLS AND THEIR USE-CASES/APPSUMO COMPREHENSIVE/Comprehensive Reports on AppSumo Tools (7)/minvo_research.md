## 5. Limitations

### Technical Limitations
- **Processing Time:** Complex videos with multiple speakers may require longer processing times
- **File Size Limits:** 5GB upload limit may restrict very long or high-resolution videos
- **Internet Dependency:** Requires stable internet connection for cloud-based processing
- **Storage Limitations:** 30-day upload storage may require regular content management
- **Platform Integration:** Some social media platforms may have API limitations affecting direct posting

### Content Limitations
- **AI Accuracy:** AI moment detection may miss context-specific important segments
- **Creative Control:** Automated editing may not match specific creative vision requirements
- **Complex Content:** Highly technical or niche content may challenge AI understanding
- **Multi-Speaker Handling:** Videos with many speakers may have less accurate moment detection
- **Visual Content:** Primarily optimized for talking-head content rather than action or visual-heavy videos

### Feature Limitations
- **Advanced Editing:** Limited compared to professional video editing software like Premiere Pro
- **Custom Effects:** Fewer advanced visual effects and transitions options
- **Audio Mixing:** Basic audio editing compared to dedicated audio production tools
- **Collaboration:** Limited team collaboration features for larger production teams
- **Version Control:** No advanced version control for iterative editing processes
