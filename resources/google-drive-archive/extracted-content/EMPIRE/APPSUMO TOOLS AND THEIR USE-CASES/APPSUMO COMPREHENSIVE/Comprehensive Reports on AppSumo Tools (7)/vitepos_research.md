## 2. Primary Features & Capabilities

### Core POS Functionality
- **Multi-Mode Operations:** Three distinct POS modes - Grocery/Retail, Restaurant (Pay First), and Restaurant (Traditional)
- **Unlimited Outlets & Counters:** Manage multiple store locations and checkout counters simultaneously
- **Offline Mode:** Continue operations during internet outages with local data storage and cloud sync
- **Real-Time Synchronization:** Seamless integration between online WooCommerce store and local POS operations
- **Multi-Language & Multi-Color Support:** Customizable interface in multiple languages and color schemes
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Secure staff management with customizable permissions and access levels

### Inventory & Stock Management
- **Full Stock Management:** Comprehensive inventory tracking across all outlets and online channels
- **Stock Transfer Between Outlets:** Move inventory between locations with approval workflows
- **Vendor Management:** Acquire new products from suppliers with purchase order functionality
- **Real-Time Stock Updates:** Automatic inventory synchronization between online and offline sales
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Automated notifications for inventory replenishment needs
- **Barcode Generation & Scanning:** Built-in barcode generator and mobile scanner support

### Sales & Payment Processing
- **Split Payment Support:** Accept multiple payment methods for single transactions
- **Stripe Payment Integration:** Secure credit card processing with Stripe gateway
- **Cash Drawer Management:** Track cash transactions with opening/closing balance reconciliation
- **Customizable Pricing:** Adjust item prices directly in cart for discounts and special offers
- **Hold Cart Functionality:** Save incomplete transactions for later completion
- **Customer Management:** Add, search, and manage customer information and purchase history

### Restaurant-Specific Features
- **Table Management:** Create, update, and manage restaurant tables with real-time status
- **Kitchen Panel:** Dedicated interface for kitchen staff to manage orders and preparation
- **Waiter Panel:** Mobile-friendly interface for waitstaff to take orders and manage tables
- **Product Addons:** Configure additional options and customizations for menu items
- **Order Modifications:** Add or remove items from active orders with real-time updates
- **Traditional vs. Pay-First Modes:** Support for different restaurant service models

### Reporting & Analytics
- **Comprehensive Sales Reports:** Detailed analytics on sales performance across all channels
- **Outlet-Specific Reporting:** Individual performance metrics for each store location
- **Tax Calculation by Outlet:** Location-based tax calculations and reporting
- **Customer Analytics:** Track customer behavior and purchase patterns
- **Inventory Reports:** Stock levels, movement, and valuation across all locations
- **Staff Performance Tracking:** Monitor individual cashier and staff productivity

### Integration & Customization
- **WooCommerce Native Integration:** Built specifically for WooCommerce with deep integration
- **HPOS (High-Performance Order Storage) Support:** Optimized for WooCommerce's latest architecture
- **RTL (Right-to-Left) Language Support:** Full support for Arabic, Hebrew, and other RTL languages
- **Custom Receipt/Invoice Design:** Branded receipts with logo and custom formatting
- **API Integration:** Connect with third-party systems and services
- **WordPress Plugin Architecture:** Seamless integration with existing WordPress/WooCommerce sites
