## 5. Limitations

### Content Limitations
- **AI Content Quality:** While high-quality, AI-generated content may occasionally require human review and editing
- **Industry Specificity:** Some highly specialized industries may need more manual content customization
- **Trending Topics:** May not automatically incorporate real-time trending topics or current events
- **Personal Touch:** Automated content lacks the personal touch of individually crafted posts
- **Content Repetition:** Extended use may lead to similar content patterns requiring manual variation

### Platform Limitations
- **Platform Updates:** Social media platform changes may temporarily affect posting capabilities
- **API Dependencies:** Relies on third-party platform APIs which can change or experience downtime
- **Content Approval:** Some platforms require manual approval for certain content types
- **Character Limits:** Must work within each platform's specific character and format limitations
- **Video Limitations:** AI video generation is basic compared to professional video production

### Technical Limitations
- **Internet Dependency:** Requires stable internet connection for content generation and scheduling
- **Learning Curve:** Initial setup and brand profile creation requires time investment
- **Customization Depth:** Advanced design customization may be limited compared to professional design tools
- **Integration Limits:** May not integrate with all business tools and CRM systems
- **Storage Limits:** Custom media library may have storage limitations for large asset collections
