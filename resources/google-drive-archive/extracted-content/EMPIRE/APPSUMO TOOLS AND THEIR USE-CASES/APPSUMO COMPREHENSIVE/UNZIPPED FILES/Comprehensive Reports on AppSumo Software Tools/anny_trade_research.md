## 4. Best Practices

### Expert-Recommended Workflows:
- **Risk Management First:** Always set position size limits (1-5% per trade) and use stop-loss automation before deploying any strategy
- **Strategy Diversification:** Combine multiple signal sources and bot strategies to reduce dependency on single market conditions
- **Backtesting Validation:** Test all strategies with at least 6 months of historical data before live deployment
- **Gradual Scaling:** Start with small positions and gradually increase allocation as strategies prove profitable
- **Community Learning:** Actively participate in signal groups to understand market dynamics and strategy rationale

### Setup Configurations:
- **Exchange Integration:** Connect with API keys that have trading permissions but disable withdrawal capabilities for security
- **Risk Parameters:** Configure maximum daily loss limits (2-5%), position sizing rules, and correlation limits between strategies
- **Notification Settings:** Set up multi-channel alerts (app, Telegram, Discord) for important trade executions and risk events
- **Portfolio Allocation:** Maintain 20-30% in stablecoins for market downturns and rebalancing opportunities
- **Strategy Selection:** Choose 3-5 complementary strategies covering different market conditions (trending, ranging, volatile)

### Communities and Resources:
- **Official Discord:** Active community with 29,000+ users sharing strategies and market insights
- **Telegram Groups:** Real-time signal sharing and strategy discussions with experienced traders
- **Strategy Marketplace:** Access to professionally developed algorithms with performance history
- **Educational Content:** Platform-provided tutorials, webinars, and strategy explanations
- **Developer Community:** Python and Node.js developers sharing custom implementations and optimizations
