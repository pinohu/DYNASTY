# ElectroNeek - Comprehensive Research Report

## 1. Overview

**Name of Software:** ElectroNeek  
**Creator/Company:** ElectroNeek Inc.  
**Website:** https://electroneek.com/  
**Summary:** ElectroNeek is an AI-powered Robotic Process Automation (RPA) platform that automates repetitive tasks across remote or local desktops, web, and SaaS environments. The platform has evolved from traditional RPA to comprehensive AI-powered automation featuring AI Agents, Document Intelligence, and seamless integrations. With over 1 million workflow runs monthly and 150+ integrations, ElectroNeek serves as a cost-effective alternative to enterprise RPA solutions like UiPath and Automation Anywhere, specifically designed for SMBs and organizations seeking accessible automation without extensive technical expertise.
