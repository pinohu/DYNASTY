1. **Early Stage Platform**: Founded in 2023, relatively new with potential stability concerns

3. **Learning Curve**: No-code doesn't mean no complexity - workflows can be intricate
4. **API Dependencies**: Reliant on third-party AI models and their availability
5. **Limited Offline Capabilities**: Requires internet connection for all operations

- **Credit Consumption**: Complex workflows can consume credits quickly
