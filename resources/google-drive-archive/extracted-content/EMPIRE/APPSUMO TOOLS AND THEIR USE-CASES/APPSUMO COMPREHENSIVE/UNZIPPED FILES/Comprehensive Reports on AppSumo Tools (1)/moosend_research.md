# Moosend - Email Marketing and Automation Platform

## Overview
Moosend is an affordable email marketing platform designed for marketers, sellers, and business owners to turn subscribers into fans and grow their businesses through email and marketing automation. The platform focuses on ease of use with drag-and-drop functionalities for email designs, newsletter creation, and custom landing page building.

## Key Features
- **Drag-and-Drop Email Editor**: Visual email builder with customizable templates
- **Marketing Automation**: Ready-made workflows and custom automation builder
- **Landing Page Builder**: Create custom landing pages without coding
- **Audience Segmentation**: Target specific groups based on CRM data
- **Subscription Forms**: Custom forms to attract and capture leads
- **A/B Testing**: Test different email variations for optimization
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Comprehensive campaign performance metrics
- **CRM Integration**: Track customer behavior and purchase history
- **Template Library**: Hundreds of business-ready email templates
- **GDPR Compliance**: Built-in compliance features for data protection
