- **File Size Limits**: Maximum 16MB per image file

- **Processing Speed**: Bulk processing may take time for very large image libraries
- **Internet Dependency**: Requires internet connection for AI processing

- **Complex Images**: May struggle with highly abstract or artistic images
- **Context Understanding**: Limited understanding of image context within content
- **Cultural Nuances**: May miss cultural or regional context in image descriptions
- **Technical Images**: May not accurately describe highly technical diagrams or charts
