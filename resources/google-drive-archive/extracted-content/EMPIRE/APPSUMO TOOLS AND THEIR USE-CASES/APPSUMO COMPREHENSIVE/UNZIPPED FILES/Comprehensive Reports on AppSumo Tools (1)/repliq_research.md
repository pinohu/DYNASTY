# RepliQ - Personalized Video Outreach Platform

## Overview
RepliQ is a video creation tool that enables users to automatically generate thousands of personalized outreach videos to boost response rates. The platform allows users to create a single video template and then generate loads of branded prospecting videos personalized for every lead, featuring custom backgrounds like websites, social media profiles, ChatGPT prompts, and job listings.

## Key Features

### Core Video Creation
- **Record or Upload Videos**: Create source videos directly on the platform or upload existing content
- **Personalized Backgrounds**: Display websites, social media profiles, ChatGPT prompts, or job listings in video backgrounds
- **Real-time Content**: Feature real-time Google keyword search results
- **Screen Sharing**: Demo software and share sales decks with screen overlay options
- **Small Bubble Overlay**: Add personal touch for cold pitches

### AI-Powered Personalization
- **ChatGPT Integration**: Generate AI responses based on custom prompts for video backgrounds
- **Dynamic Variables**: Automatically insert prospect information into videos
- **Bulk Processing**: Process lead data to generate thousands of personalized videos
- **Personalized Thumbnails**: Customize video thumbnails for each prospect

### Customization & Branding
- **Remove RepliQ Branding**: White-label the platform with your own branding
- **Custom Logo & Domain**: Add company logo and use custom domain
- **CTA Buttons**: Add custom call-to-action buttons redirecting to landing pages, booking pages, and opt-in forms
- **Design Customization**: Point-and-click editor to switch between video backgrounds and presentations
- **CNAME Support**: Full domain customization capabilities

### Integration & Distribution
- **Email & LinkedIn Embedding**: Embed videos directly in emails or LinkedIn messages
- **Outreach Tool Integration**: Connect with Salesflow, QuickMail, and Mixmax
- **Full API Access**: Complete API for custom integrations
- **HTML Code Sharing**: Get HTML embed codes for emails and social media
- **Dynamic Video Links**: Generate unique video links for each prospect

### Additional Features
- **Personalized Images**: Create custom images alongside videos
- **Merge Feature**: Combine multiple videos into single presentations
- **LinkedIn Profile Backgrounds**: Feature prospect's LinkedIn profiles in videos
- **Dashboard Management**: Access and manage all personalized videos from central dashboard
- **Preview Functionality**: Review finished products before sending

## Conclusion

The tool is best suited for sales professionals and marketing teams who can leverage bulk personalization capabilities and have the resources to implement effective video outreach strategies.

The platform's strength lies in its ability to automate personalization at scale while maintaining professional quality and brand consistency. However, potential users should carefully consider the feature limitations of the AppSumo version and evaluate whether the available functionality meets their specific needs and expectations.

For organizations committed to video-based outreach and willing to invest time in mastering the platform, RepliQ can provide substantial value through improved response rates and streamlined personalization workflows. The lifetime access model eliminates ongoing costs, making it an attractive option for long-term users despite the feature constraints.
