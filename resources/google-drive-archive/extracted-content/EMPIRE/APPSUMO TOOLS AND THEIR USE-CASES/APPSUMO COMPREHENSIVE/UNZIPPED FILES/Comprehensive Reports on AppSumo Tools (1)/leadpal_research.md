# LeadPal - Social Lead Generation Platform Research

## Overview
LeadPal is a cloud-based social lead generation platform that revolutionizes email list building by enabling one-click opt-ins through social media accounts. The platform eliminates traditional opt-in forms that can drive away up to 72% of potential subscribers, instead allowing users to capture verified email addresses through social logins from major platforms including Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, Twitter, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft, and Yahoo. LeadPal addresses the critical issue of fake email submissions by leveraging social network authentication to ensure lead quality and authenticity.
