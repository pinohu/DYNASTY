# Lifetime Deal to Notion Mastery - Research

## Overview
The "Lifetime Deal to Notion Mastery" refers to Marie Poulin and Benjamin Borowski's comprehensive Notion training program, delivered entirely through Notion itself. This flagship program is designed for solo practitioners, entrepreneurs, and small business owners who want to master life and business workflows using Notion. The program combines structured curriculum with live training, community support, and ongoing updates. Marie Poulin is a Notion Ambassador and Certified Consultant, while Benjamin Borowski is a Notion Certified Consultant and Formula expert. The program is currently priced at $799 for one year of access, with payment plans available.

The "Lifetime Deal to Notion Mastery" represents a premium educational investment for serious Notion users seeking comprehensive business system mastery. The program's focus on practical application and real-world system building ensures that students develop immediately applicable skills. The community aspect provides ongoing learning opportunities and professional networking that extends well beyond the formal program period.
