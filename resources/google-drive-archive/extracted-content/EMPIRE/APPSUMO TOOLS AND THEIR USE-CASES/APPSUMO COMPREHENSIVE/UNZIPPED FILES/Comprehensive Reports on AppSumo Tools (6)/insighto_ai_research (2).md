## 5. Limitations

### Known Issues & Disadvantages:

**Technical Limitations:**
- Voice quality dependent on internet connection stability
- GPT-4 usage requires 20x credits compared to GPT-3.5
- ElevenLabs voice synthesis costs 10x more than Azure Speech
- Complex integrations may require technical expertise despite "no-code" positioning

**Platform Constraints:**
- Limited to 60-day code redemption period for AppSumo users
- Credit-based pricing model can become expensive for high-volume usage
- Voice agent latency may vary based on geographic location
- Some advanced features require higher-tier plans

**Competitive Disadvantages:**
- Newer platform compared to established competitors like Intercom or Drift
- Smaller template library compared to ManyChat or Chatfuel
- Limited third-party app ecosystem compared to major platforms
- Voice agent technology still evolving, may have occasional recognition issues

**LTD vs Premium Limitations:**
- AppSumo LTD users limited to Gold Plan features
