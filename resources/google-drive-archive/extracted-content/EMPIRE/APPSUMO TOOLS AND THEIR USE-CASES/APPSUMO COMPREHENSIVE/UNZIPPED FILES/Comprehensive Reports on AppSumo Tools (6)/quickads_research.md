# Quickads - Comprehensive Research Report

## 1. Overview

**Name:** Quickads (QuickAds.ai)
**Creator/Company:** 88 Ventures US, LLC (Founded August 5, 2023)
**Website:** https://www.quickads.ai/
**AppSumo Deal Status:** ACTIVE (6 tiers: $69-459, up to unlimited features)

**Core Purpose:** Quickads is an AI-powered ad creation and analysis platform that helps marketers discover winning ad strategies, analyze competitor campaigns, and generate high-converting image and video advertisements in seconds. The platform combines a massive database of 10+ million analyzed ads with advanced AI generation capabilities, enabling users to create professional-quality ad creatives across multiple formats and platforms. It streamlines the entire ad creation workflow from inspiration discovery to final publication, incorporating features like virtual photoshoots, AI avatars, voice generation, and direct social media scheduling.

**Problem It Solves:** Traditional ad creation requires extensive market research, expensive design resources, lengthy production timelines, and guesswork about what creative elements will perform well. Most businesses struggle with identifying winning ad strategies, creating professional-quality visuals, generating engaging video content, and scaling ad production across multiple campaigns and platforms. Quickads eliminates these barriers by providing instant access to proven ad strategies, AI-powered creative generation, comprehensive analysis tools, and automated production workflows that reduce ad creation time from days to minutes while improving performance through data-driven insights.
