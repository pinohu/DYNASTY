## Executive Summary

Blurweb App is a browser extension designed to blur sensitive information on web pages during screen recording or live screen sharing. This tool eliminates the need for post-production video editing by allowing users to blur elements in real-time. With over 10,000 installs and excellent user reviews, it has proven valuable for content creators, educators, and professionals who frequently share their screens.
