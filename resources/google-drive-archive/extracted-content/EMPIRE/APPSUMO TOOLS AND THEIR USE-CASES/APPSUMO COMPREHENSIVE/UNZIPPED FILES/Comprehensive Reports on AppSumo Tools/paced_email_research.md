- **Email Aliases**: Create unlimited email aliases to protect privacy

- **Custom Rules**: Apply rules for inbound emails (ignore, forward, block)
- **Browser Extensions**: Available for Safari, Chrome, Edge, and Firefox
- **Burner Emails**: Generate temporary email addresses
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Security with authenticator apps or FIDO U2F
- **Custom Domains**: Add your own domain names or create pseudonyms
- **Bulk Response**: Reply to multiple emails when digest arrives

- **Third-Party Integrations**: APIs, webhooks, Zapier, Integrately, Pabbly Connect

- **CNAME Support**: Custom domain configuration
