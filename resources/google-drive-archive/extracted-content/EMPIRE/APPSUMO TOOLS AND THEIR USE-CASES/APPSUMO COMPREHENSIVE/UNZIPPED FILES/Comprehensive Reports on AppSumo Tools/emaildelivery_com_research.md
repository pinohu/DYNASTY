# EmailDelivery.com Research

## Product Overview
**EmailDelivery.com** is a self-hosted email service provider (ESP) that allows users to send custom email campaigns from their own IP addresses for free. It's designed as a complete email marketing solution for agencies and businesses wanting to bring their email marketing in-house.

## Product Features

### Core Capabilities:
- **Self-Hosted ESP**: Run your own email service provider like Mailchimp
- **Own IP Addresses**: Send emails using your own IPs for free
- **Unlimited Sending**: No limits on domains, brands, contacts, or sending
- **White-Label Ready**: Offer as paid service to clients

### Advanced Email Features:
- **Multiple ESPs/MTAs**: Route email through multiple providers for optimal deliverability
- **Load Balancing**: Sophisticated routing to improve sender reputation
- **IP Warmup**: Automated IP warming processes
- **Throttling Controls**: Set daily/hourly sending limits
- **SMTP Relay & APIs**: Integration with Mailgun, Amazon SES, Bird (SparkPost)

### Campaign Management:
- **Advanced Segmentation**: Send to multiple lists, tags, and segments simultaneously
- **Automated Sequences**: Create drip campaigns and follow-up sequences
- **Transactional Email**: Welcome messages, shipping notifications, password resets
- **Real-time Reporting**: Opens, clicks, and engagement analytics

### Design & Forms:
- **Drag-and-Drop Builder**: Responsive email design from templates or scratch
- **Multiple Composers**: Drag-and-drop, WYSIWYG, and raw HTML options
- **Opt-in Forms**: Pop-ups, slide-outs, floating bars, embedded forms
- **Exit Intent**: Capture emails as users leave your site

### Integrations:
- **APIs**: REST API and Webhooks
- **Platforms**: WordPress, Zapier integration
- **Media**: Pixabay integration for images
- **Technical**: CNAME support, GDPR compliant
