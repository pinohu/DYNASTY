## Key Takeaways
Payhip represents a case where a product was listed on AppSumo's system as "coming soon" but never actually launched. This creates confusion as it appears in searches and may be mistakenly included in owned product lists.

The platform itself is a legitimate and well-regarded e-commerce solution for digital products, with features comparable to other platforms in the space. However, it operates on traditional subscription pricing and has never offered lifetime deals through AppSumo.

This highlights the importance of distinguishing between products that were actually sold on AppSumo versus those that were merely considered or listed as potential deals. The "sold out" status on the page is misleading - it should indicate "never launched."

For users looking for e-commerce solutions, there are several actual AppSumo alternatives that have been successfully launched and sold on the platform.
