# Mixo - AI-Powered Landing Page Builder

## Overview
Mixo is an AI-powered landing page builder designed specifically for entrepreneurs, agencies, and freelancers to create and launch websites in under 30 seconds. The platform focuses on simplicity and speed, generating SEO-optimized landing pages from just a one-sentence description of your business idea.

## Key Features
- **AI Website Generation**: Creates complete landing pages in seconds from brief descriptions
- **No-Code Editor**: Drag-and-drop interface requiring no coding or design skills
- **Integrated Waiting List**: Built-in email capture and lead management system
- **Mobile-First Design**: Conversion-optimized, responsive templates
- **Fast Hosting**: Serverless infrastructure for scalable performance
- **Custom Domains**: Connect your own domain with free SSL certificates
- **SEO Optimization**: Built-in search engine optimization features
- **Unsplash Integration**: Access to stock photos directly in the editor
- **Export Capabilities**: CSV export to Google Sheets and Excel
- **White Label Options**: Custom branding capabilities
