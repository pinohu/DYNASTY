## Product Description
Castmagic is an AI-powered repurposing platform designed to help agencies and content teams transform long-form audio and video content into shareable content assets. The platform uses artificial intelligence to transcribe, analyze, and generate various types of written content from podcasts, videos, webinars, and other long-form media.

The tool addresses a common challenge for content creators and agencies: the time-consuming process of manually extracting value from long-form content. Instead of spending hours creating blog posts, social media content, and marketing materials from existing media, Castmagic automates this process using AI technology.
