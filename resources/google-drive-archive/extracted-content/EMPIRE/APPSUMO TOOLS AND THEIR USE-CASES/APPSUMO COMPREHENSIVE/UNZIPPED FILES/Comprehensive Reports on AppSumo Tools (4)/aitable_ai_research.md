## Product Description
AITable.ai is an AI-powered platform that lets you build powerful database apps for better project management and CRMs. It serves as an alternative to Airtable, HubSpot, and Monday.com, offering spreadsheet-style databases with AI capabilities.

## Key Features

### Core Functionality
- **AI-Powered Database Creation**: Build custom databases with AI assistance
- **Spreadsheet-Style Interface**: Familiar Excel-like interface with advanced database capabilities
- **Multiple View Types**: Kanban boards, Gantt charts, galleries, and visual dashboards
- **Form Generation**: Turn any datasheet into online forms with custom permissions
- **QR Code Integration**: Connect shareable QR codes to forms for in-person data collection

### AI Capabilities
- **ChatGPT-Powered Chatbots**: Create AI agents trained on company data
- **AI Sales Lead Collection**: Automated lead generation and management
- **Optimized AI Chatbot Dataset Insights**: Advanced analytics for chatbot performance
- **All Future AI Models Included**: Access to new AI models as they're released

### Integration & Automation
- **6,000+ App Integrations**: Connect via Zapier, Make, and Activepieces
- **Slack Integration**: Automated notifications and updates
- **WordPress & Wix Integration**: Embed chatbots and forms on websites
- **API Access**: Basic API, Contacts API, and 20 API QPS
