## Product Description
Cheqbook is fast, easy accounting software with powerful automation and included invoicing designed for individuals and small businesses. The platform focuses on saving time through automated import and categorization of expenses, positioning itself as an alternative to more expensive accounting solutions.

## Key Features
### Core Accounting Features:
- **AccuScore Smart Categorization**: AI-powered automatic transaction categorization
- **Automated Bank Import**: Automatically download and categorize transactions
- **Unlimited Simultaneous Users**: Create unlimited user accounts
- **Journal Entries & Split Transactions**: Professional accounting capabilities
- **Fast Bank Reconciliation**: Streamlined reconciliation process

### Business Management:
- **Invoicing & Online Payments**: Send invoices and accept payments online
- **Bill Management**: Manage bills and print checks
- **15 Financial Reports**: Including Balance Sheet and P&L
- **Mobile Access**: View books from iPad or Android tablet
- **Data Export**: Export contacts and transactions

### Professional Features:

- **Print Reports & 1099s**: Professional reporting capabilities
- **Bulk Transaction Import**: Import transactions in bulk
- **Automated Daily Backup**: Secure offsite backup
- **Lock Books**: Secure your financial data
- **Free Training**: Videos, webinars, and certification

### Business Scaling:
- **Unlimited Archived Companies**: Manage multiple business entities
- **QuickBooks Import**: Import key data from QuickBooks for easier transition

The comprehensive feature set and unlimited users make it valuable for growing teams, but the mixed reviews suggest careful evaluation during the trial period is essential.
