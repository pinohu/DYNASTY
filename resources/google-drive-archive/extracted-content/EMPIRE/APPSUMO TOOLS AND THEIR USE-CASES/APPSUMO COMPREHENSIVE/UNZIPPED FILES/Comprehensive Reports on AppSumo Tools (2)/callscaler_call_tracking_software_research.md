## Product Description
CallScaler is a comprehensive call tracking software designed for marketing and SEO agencies, local businesses, and lead generation companies. The platform makes it simple to get call tracking numbers and track inbound calls with teams, clients, or affiliates, helping businesses understand how customers find them and which marketing campaigns are driving phone calls.

The software provides auto-magical tracking of marketing campaigns driving calls to businesses, allowing users to easily assign names to numbers based on their marketing campaigns and see how every marketing channel performs. CallScaler processes over 1 million minutes annually, demonstrating its scale and reliability in the call tracking industry.
