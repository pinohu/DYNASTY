# AI Toolbar Research Report

## Overview
- **Name**: AI Toolbar
- **Creator/Company**: Chris_AIToolbar / AI Toolbar Ltd (UK)
- **Website**: https://www.aitoolbar.co.uk/
- **Core Purpose**: Chrome extension that serves as an AI-powered virtual assistant, enabling users to perform AI tasks directly within their browser. AI Toolbar allows users to highlight text on any webpage and execute AI commands through voice activation, text prompts, or pre-built templates, effectively turning any browser into an AI-powered workspace.
