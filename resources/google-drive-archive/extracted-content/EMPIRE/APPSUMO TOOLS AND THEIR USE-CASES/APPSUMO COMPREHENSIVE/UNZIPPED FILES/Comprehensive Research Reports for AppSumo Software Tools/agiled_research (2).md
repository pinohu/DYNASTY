# Agiled Research Report

## Overview

**Name of software:** Agiled
**Creator/Company:** Agiled.app
**Website:** https://agiled.app/
**Summary:** Agiled is a comprehensive all-in-one business management platform designed for small businesses, offering CRM, project management, invoicing, contracts, proposals, time tracking, team management, and AI-powered features in a single integrated solution.

## Primary Features & Capabilities

### Core Functionalities
- **CRM & Lead Management:** Unlimited leads, clients, and contacts with sales pipeline management
- **Project Management:** Task allocation, project templates, time tracking, and collaboration tools
- **Financial Management:** Invoicing, estimates, contracts, proposals, subscription billing, and payment processing
- **Team Management:** Employee management, leave tracking, attendance monitoring, and payroll
- **Client Portal:** White-labeled client access with custom branding and domain
- **Document Management:** File sharing, e-signatures, contract builder, and proposal creation
- **Scheduling:** Appointment booking, calendar management, and availability control
- **Business Automations:** Workflow automation, task automation, and customer onboarding flows
- **Agiled AI:** AI-powered content creation for contracts, proposals, ads, emails, and project descriptions

### Integrations
- **Accounting:** QuickBooks, Xero integrations
- **Email:** Custom SMTP, email templates, and automated notifications
- **Payment Processing:** Multiple payment gateways for invoices and subscriptions
- **Communication:** Real-time chat, announcements, and file sharing
- **Mobile Apps:** Available on Android & iOS for business management on-the-go
- **API Access:** Available for custom integrations and third-party connections

### AI & Automation Capabilities
- **Agiled AI:** Content generation for business documents, marketing materials, and project descriptions
- **Workflow Automation:** Custom automated workflows for repetitive business processes
- **Smart Templates:** Pre-built templates for forms, projects, contracts, and proposals
- **Automated Onboarding:** Customer onboarding flows with automatic task assignment

### Mobile & Desktop Support
- **Web Application:** Full-featured browser-based platform
- **Mobile Apps:** Native Android and iOS applications
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for all device sizes
- **Offline Capability:** Limited offline functionality for mobile apps

## Limitations

### Known Issues & Disadvantages
- **Bug Reports:** Users report occasional bugs and glitches, particularly in newer features (noted in Zendo review)
- **Learning Curve:** Comprehensive feature set can be overwhelming for new users initially
- **Limited Advanced Reporting:** Basic reporting capabilities compared to specialized business intelligence tools
- **No Native Agiled AI in Lower Tiers:** AI features only available in highest tier (10 codes)
- **Storage Limitations:** Storage caps range from 100GB to 1TB depending on tier

### Tier-Specific Limitations (AppSumo LTD)
- **Tier 1 (1 code):** 5 team members, 100GB storage, 5 automations, 2 pipelines
- **Tier 2 (2 codes):** 15 team members, 200GB storage, 10 automations, 4 pipelines  
- **Tier 3 (3 codes):** 25 team members, 300GB storage, 15 automations, 6 pipelines
- **Tiers 4-9:** Scaling team members (35-85), storage (400GB-900GB), pipelines (8-18)
- **Tier 10 (10 codes):** Unlimited team members, 1TB storage, 20 pipelines, Agiled AI included

### Comparison Disadvantages
- **Feature Depth:** Individual modules may lack depth compared to specialized tools (e.g., advanced CRM features vs. Salesforce)
