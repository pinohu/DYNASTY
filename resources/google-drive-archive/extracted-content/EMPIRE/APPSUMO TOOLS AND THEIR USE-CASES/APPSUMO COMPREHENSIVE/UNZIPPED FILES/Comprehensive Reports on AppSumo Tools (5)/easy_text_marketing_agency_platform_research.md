- **Frequency Management**: Balance engagement with avoiding message fatigue
- **Value-First Approach**: Provide value in every message to maintain engagement
- **Clear Call-to-Actions**: Include clear, actionable next steps in messages

- **Brand Voice**: Maintain consistent brand voice across all messages

- **Cross-Channel Promotion**: Promote SMS opt-ins across other marketing channels
- **Event-Based Collection**: Collect contacts at events and in-person interactions

### Compliance & Legal Considerations
