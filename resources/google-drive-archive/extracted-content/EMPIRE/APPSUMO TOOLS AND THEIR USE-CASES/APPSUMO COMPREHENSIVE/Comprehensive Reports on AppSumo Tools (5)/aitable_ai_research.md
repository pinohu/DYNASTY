- **Visual Database Interface**: Hybrid spreadsheet-database that feels like Excel but with database power

- **Multiple View Types**: Kanban boards, Gantt charts, galleries, grid view, and custom dashboards
- **Column Types**: Assignees, deadlines, categories, attachments, and multimedia support
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Multiple users can work simultaneously on data

### AI-Powered Capabilities
- **AI Chatbots**: Create ChatGPT-powered chatbots trained on your company data
- **AI Customer Service**: 24/7 automated customer support with conversational or Q&A flows

- **AI Data Insights**: Optimized AI chatbot dataset insights and analytics
- **Custom AI Models**: Access to all future AI models included

### Form Creation & Data Collection
- **One-Click Forms**: Instantly turn any datasheet into an online form
- **Custom Permissions**: Require login, limit submissions, set access controls
- **QR Code Integration**: Generate QR codes for forms to collect data at events
- **Pre-filled Content**: Customize forms with pre-filled data for channel tracking
- **Website Integration**: Embed forms directly into websites

- **6,000+ App Integrations**: Connect via Zapier, Make, Pabbly, Activepieces, and Albato
