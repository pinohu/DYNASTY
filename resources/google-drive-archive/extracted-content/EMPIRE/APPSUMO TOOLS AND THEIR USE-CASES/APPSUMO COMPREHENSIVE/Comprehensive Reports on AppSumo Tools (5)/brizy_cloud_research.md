- **Hosting Dependency:** Reliant on Brizy's hosting infrastructure
- **Feature Limitations:** Limited to features provided by the platform
- **Customization Constraints:** Advanced customizations may be limited

### Pricing Structure Considerations
- **Subscription Model:** Ongoing monthly costs for continued access
- **Website Limits:** Limited number of websites per plan tier
- **Additional Site Costs:** Extra charges for additional websites beyond plan limits
- **Feature Restrictions:** Higher-tier features require more expensive plans
