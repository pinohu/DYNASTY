## Historical Context
Fouita Smart Widgets was one of AppSumo's most successful widget/popup deals, achieving an exceptional 4.97/5 star rating from 39 reviews. The platform distinguished itself by offering a comprehensive widget creation solution that was both powerful and user-friendly. Users consistently praised its ease of use, with many noting there was "no learning curve" and that it made widget creation and implementation remarkably simple. The inline editing feature was particularly appreciated, allowing users to see exactly how their widgets would appear to visitors. Performance was another standout aspect, with users reporting that the widgets were fast, lightweight, and had no negative impact on page loading speeds. The behavioral targeting capabilities set it apart from simpler popup tools, allowing sophisticated conditional display based on visitor interactions, location, source, and other factors. The stackable pricing model was well-received, though many users later regretted not purchasing higher tiers when they realized the platform's value. Customer support received consistent praise, with users describing it as "top-notch." The deal's popularity led to it selling out, with users expressing disappointment when they tried to upgrade their plans only to find the deal had ended. This level of user satisfaction and regret about missing higher tiers is a strong indicator of the platform's quality and value proposition. For users who missed this deal, the challenge is finding alternatives that match Fouita's combination of ease of use, performance, and sophisticated targeting capabilities.
