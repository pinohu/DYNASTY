## Key Takeaways
Notice represents a classic example of startup failure in the lifetime deal space. Despite having a good product concept and positive initial user feedback, the company's inability to sustain its business model led to breaking promises to AppSumo customers. The company's decision to blame customers for "abuse" while forcing them to pay again for previously purchased lifetime access demonstrates poor business ethics and accountability.

This case highlights the risks inherent in lifetime deals from early-stage companies and the importance of AppSumo's "We Got Your Back" protection for customers.
