## Historical AppSumo Deal (December 2016)
- **Launch Date**: December 2016
- **Deal Duration**: 2 weeks

## Key Takeaways
Paperform represents one of AppSumo's greatest success stories. The December 2016 lifetime deal for $39 was instrumental in launching what became a $5.4M+ ARR business. The deal sold nearly 3,000 units and provided the founders with enough capital and user validation to quit their day jobs and focus full-time on the business.

The AppSumo deal served multiple purposes beyond just revenue:
1. **Market Validation**: 3,000 customers proved there was demand
2. **User Feedback**: Early adopters shaped the product roadmap
3. **Seed Funding**: $35K provided runway for full-time development
4. **Product Hunt Launch**: Coordinated launch created additional momentum

Today, Paperform is a successful, independent SaaS company that competes effectively with established players like Typeform. While no longer available on AppSumo, it demonstrates how lifetime deals can be foundational for startups when executed properly.

The company's success story shows that AppSumo deals, when done right, can be mutually beneficial - providing customers with excellent value while giving startups the resources and validation needed to build sustainable businesses.
