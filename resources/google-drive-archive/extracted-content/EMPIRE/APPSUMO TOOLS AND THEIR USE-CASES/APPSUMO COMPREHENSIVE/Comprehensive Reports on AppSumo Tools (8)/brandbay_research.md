## 5. Limitations

### Technical and Performance Constraints
- **Storage Limits:** Tier 1 (100GB), Tier 2 (400GB), Tier 3 (1TB) may be insufficient for video-heavy workflows
- **File Size Restrictions:** Large video files and high-resolution assets may face upload limitations
- **Browser Dependency:** Chrome extension limits cross-browser workflow integration
- **Internet Connectivity:** Cloud-based platform requires reliable internet for optimal performance
- **Mobile Experience:** Limited mobile app functionality compared to desktop and browser versions

### Feature and Functionality Gaps
- **Advanced Editing:** No built-in image or video editing capabilities within the platform
- **Automated Workflows:** Limited automation compared to enterprise digital asset management solutions
- **Analytics Depth:** Basic usage analytics compared to comprehensive DAM platforms
- **Integration Limitations:** Limited direct integrations with design software and marketing tools
- **Advanced Search:** Basic search functionality compared to AI-powered asset discovery systems

### Business and Operational Limitations
- **Learning Curve:** Initial setup and organization requires time investment and strategic planning
- **User Adoption:** Team members may resist changing from existing file storage habits
- **Scalability Concerns:** Performance may degrade with very large asset libraries or high concurrent usage
- **Backup Dependencies:** Relies on platform's backup systems without local redundancy options
- **Migration Complexity:** Moving existing asset libraries requires significant time and organization effort

### Market and Competitive Positioning
- **Enterprise Features:** Lacks advanced enterprise features like workflow automation and approval processes
- **Industry Specialization:** Generic solution may miss specialized features for specific industries
- **Advanced Security:** Basic security features compared to enterprise-grade DAM solutions
- **API Limitations:** Limited API functionality for custom integrations and advanced workflows
- **Global Capabilities:** Limited international features compared to global enterprise solutions
