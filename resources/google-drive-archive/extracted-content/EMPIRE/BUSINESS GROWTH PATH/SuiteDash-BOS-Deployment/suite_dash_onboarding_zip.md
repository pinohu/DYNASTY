## Instructions
1. Unzip the package.
2. Customize templates and SOPs for your brand.
3. Record videos using Loom, Synthesia, or Supademo.
4. Upload SOPs & Checklists to SuiteDash.
5. Apply FLOW & Checklist templates.
6. Assign Onboarding FLOW upon staff creation.
7. Monitor onboarding progress in dashboards.

*Note: Ensure you maintain naming conventions and permission hierarchies when uploading to SuiteDash for consistency and automation triggers.*
